## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Continuance of R&D for further integration of modern technology in our therapy services.** *
We have a dedicated research team focused on publishing research to enhance the efficacy of therapy. We intend to leverage the power of data, collected through numerous interventions, assessments and consultation and integrate the use of AI/ ML and other modern technology available in our therapy services. The data collected by us so far will help us advance the intervention process and programs and also help improve the efficiency of goal plans through the adoption of predictive modelling.
In furtherance of this strategy, we propose to utilize the proceeds raised from Issue to set up six  *“Centres of Excellence* *and Research” (“*   * **COER** *   *”)*  . These  *“COERs”*  will enable our Company to validate new approaches, improve service quality, and disseminate best practices across all centres, contributing to measurable improvements in outcomes and
operational excellence. For further details, please see “  *Our Business*    *- Our Centres*  ” and “  *Objects of the Issue*  ” on pages 235 and 115.
