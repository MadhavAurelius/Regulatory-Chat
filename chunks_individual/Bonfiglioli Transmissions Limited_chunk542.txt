## * **Net Asset Value per Equity Share** *

NAV per Equity Share is calculated as net worth as at the end of the period/year divided by number of equity shares outstanding as at the end of period / year plus bonus shares  *for all periods presented in accordance with* *principles of IND AS 33 -  Earnings per Share.*  The bonus shares are issued by our Company in the proportion of 1 (One) bonus Equity Share for every 2 (Two) Equity Shares of the Company pursuant to a resolution dated November 10, 2025 passed by the Shareholders and such bonus issue of equity shares were allotted pursuant to a resolution passed by Board of Directors on January 25, 2026.
