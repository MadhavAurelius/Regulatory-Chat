## **Manufacturing Facilities**

As of September 30, 2025, we had three manufacturing facilities that had a combined available annual manufacturing capacity of 1,119,652 hours in Fiscal 2024, of which two were located in Tamil Nadu (in Thirumudivakkam and Mannur) and one in Maharashtra (in Talegaon). Our manufacturing facility in Thirumudivakkam has been operational since 1999 and underwent substantial expansion in 2018, bringing its available annual capacity to 942,227 hours in Fiscal 2024. Our manufacturing facility in Mannur was established in 2011, with an available annual capacity of 48,320 hours in Fiscal 2024. Our manufacturing facility in Talegaon, Pune was established in 2023, with an available annual capacity of 129,105 hours in Fiscal 2024.
