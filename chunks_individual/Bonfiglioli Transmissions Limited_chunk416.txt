## **Key terms of other subsisting material agreements**

As on the date of this Draft Red Herring Prospectus, except as disclosed below, our Company has not entered into any other subsisting material agreements including with strategic partners, joint venture partners, and/or financial partners other than in the ordinary course of business of our Company:
* **License for limited scope manufacturing agreement between Bonfiglioli S.p.A., and our Company, dated June** * * **1, 2021, read with the amendments thereof (the “License Agreement”)** *
Pursuant to the License Agreement, which is effective from January 1, 2021, Bonfiglioli S.p.A., one of our Corporate Promoters, has granted our Company a non-exclusive, non-transferable, and non-sublicensable license to manufacture and sell certain licensed products under its intellectual property including the “Bonfiglioli” trade name, trademarks, patents, drawings, utility models, design rights, know-how pertaining to the products as stated in the License Agreement. The license permits limited-scope manufacturing and sale of the licensed products in India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, and Nepal, and subject to Bonfiglioli S.p.A.’s consent, any other country. As consideration, our Company shall pay a royalty equal to 6% of its yearly net sales (excluding intercompany sales to the Bonfiglioli S.p.A. and net of discounts and incentives). The License Agreement has a validity of one year and is automatically renewed for successive one-year terms unless terminated by either party by way of a notice  at least three months prior to the end of the Fiscal Year.
* **Intercompany Service Agreement between Bonfiglioli S.p.A., our Company and other entities forming part of** * * **the Bonfiglioli Group, dated January 1, 2024** *
Pursuant to the Intercompany Service Agreement (“  **ISA**  ”), dated January 1, 2024, Bonfiglioli S.p.A. provides certain services to our Company (among the other entities forming part of the Bonfiglioli Group) using Bonfiglioli S.p.A.’s own resources, resources available with other entities forming part of the Bonfiglioli Group, or services provided by third parties, and is authorized to enter into necessary third-party or intragroup contracts to perform such services. The scope of services include, among others, (1) corporate services such as those in respect of information technology, human capital and resources, legal and corporate affairs, administration and finance, strategic purchasing, quality, health, safety and engineering, internal and external communication, and aftersales; (2) business unit services for industry and automation solutions such as general management, analyse and provide support in product marketing, customer experience and pricing, ensuring governance and execution of projects and product launch, guaranteeing achievement of sales and business development goals, supervise manufacturing operations, contribute towards commodity’s purchasing strategy, and provide support for the optimisation of the global supply chain management; and (3) business unit services for mobility and wind industries such as general management, product marketing, coordinating and monitoring projects and product launch, sales and business development, manufacturing operations and purchasing. As consideration, our Company (and other recipients of the services from Bonfiglioli S.p.A. which are entities forming part of the Bonfiglioli Group) shall pay service fees to Bonfiglioli S.p.A. which shall be determined in accordance with the ISA, which is typically ranges from 30%-100% of the service costs incurred and determined based on fixed costs or turnover (net of taxes), with an arm’s-length mark-up of 5%, invoiced quarterly against the estimated annual fees. The ISA has a validity of one year and is automatically renewed for successive one-year terms unless terminated by way of a notice of at least 30 days before the year-end.
