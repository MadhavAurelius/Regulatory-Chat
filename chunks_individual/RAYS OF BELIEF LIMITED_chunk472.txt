#### * **Our Comprehensive Five-Phase Care Model** *

*Phase 4: Therapy Intervention and Parent Training*
This is the execution phase of the program. We deliver multidisciplinary therapy sessions through our centres or through digital mode, as per the IEP or IGP prepared specifically for the child. Key highlights under this phase include:
i.  Speech therapy, occupational therapy, ABA, developmental therapy, and special education; ii.  We conduct sessions with parents, wherein we train the caregivers on behaviour management, academic structuring, sensory regulation; and iii.  Access to customized play kits, therapy tools, and digital learning materials, designed to enhance skill-building at home.
Our clinical professionals issue monthly plan and progress reports, updating goal plans based on observed outcomes. Therapists collaborate across disciplines to ensure integrated care.
*Phase 5: Goal Attainment, Skill Development, and Inclusion*
This phase focuses on evaluating progress, celebrating milestones, and facilitating mainstream integration. As children reach developmental targets, our team:
i.  Revisits the initial assessment to measure gains across developmental domains; ii.  Prepares transition plans which include training for school, social group inclusion, and age-appropriate independence; and iii.  Supports families in working with schools, paediatricians, and local institutions to ensure continued support.
