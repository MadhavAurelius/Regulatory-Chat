In the past, there have been certain instances statutory and regulatory non-compliances and delays. There have been delays in filing the statutory forms due to operational and technical difficulties faced by the Company, however the same were appropriately filed after payment of late fees.
Post the incorporation of our Company in 2017, certain documents filed by us with the RoC namely, the forms DPT- 3, MSME-I, MGT-14 and BEN-2 were delayed.
While no show cause notice in respect of the above has been received by the Company till date, it cannot be assured that there will be no such instances in the future or that our Company will not inadvertently commit any further delays in relation to its reporting requirements or that any penalty or fine will not be imposed by any regulatory authority in respect of the same.
Further, our Company had adopted a SAR Scheme in the past, which was subsequently rescinded on August 05, 2025, by way of a shareholders resolution. The resolution for adoption of the SAR Scheme was passed as an ordinary resolution, instead of a special resolution as mandated under the SEBI (Share Based Employee Benefits and Sweat Equity) Regulations, 2021, as amended. Although the SAR Scheme has since been rescinded and no benefits were availed by any employee pursuant to the grants issued by virtue of the SAR Scheme, there can be no assurance that SEBI or any other regulatory authority will not take adverse action in the future in relation to such past non- compliance.
