## **4. Brand awareness and inclusive outreach programs.**

Our Company believes our marketing strategies have been highly effective in spreading awareness and accordingly, building trust with the community which our Company intends to serve.
The marketing expenses incurred by the Company shall be consistent with historical expenditure patterns and shall not exceed 2% of the total revenue for any Fiscal Year. Such expenditures, being funded by public monies, shall be subject to review for alignment with prior marketing spend practices. In accordance with SEBI guidelines, the Company shall disclose the nature of any tie-ups, agreements, or partnerships undertaken for the purpose of marketing expenses. Our primary marketing efforts are driven through strategic partnerships with organizations such as Google, META, Facebook, and similar platforms. Secondary marketing includes pamphlet distribution, while the third tier focuses on doctor meetings and related activities. While we do not organize doctor conferences, we actively participate in them to enhance our brand presence and engage directly with the medical community. For further details, please see  *“Our Business - Marketing and Outreach Strategy”*  and  *“Our Business - Key Outreach Pillars”*  on pages 245 and 246.
