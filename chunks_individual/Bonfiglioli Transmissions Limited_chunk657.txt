## **Short-term leases and leases of low-value assets**
We apply the short-term lease recognition exemption to our short-term leases of buildings and others (i.e., those leases that have a lease term of 12 months or less from the commencement date and do not contain a purchase option). Lease payments on short term leases and leases of low-value assets are recognized as expense on a straight-line basis over the lease term.
