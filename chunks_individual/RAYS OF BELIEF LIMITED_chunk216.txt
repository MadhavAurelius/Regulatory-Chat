#### **Rays of Belief Limited Employee Stock Option Plan 2025**
Our Company, pursuant to the resolutions passed by our Board in its meeting dated May 19, 2025 and our Shareholders in its meeting dated June 13, 2025, adopted Rays of Belief Limited Employee Stock Option Plan 2025 which, was further amended by our Board in its meeting dated August 01, 2025 and our Shareholders in its meeting dated August 05, 2025, and is in compliance with the Companies Act, 2013 and Securities and Exchange Board of India (Share Based Employee Benefits and Sweat Equity) Regulations, 2021, as amended.
