## * **Fiscal 2024 compared to Fiscal 2023** *

In Fiscal 2024, we recorded a one-time income from secondary adjustment as per transfer pricing regulations amounting to ₹140.63 million. The income was recognized further to the settlement of a dispute with the Indian income tax authorities under the Vivad se Vishwas scheme with respect to transfer pricing adjustments by the Company. As the dispute with the income tax department was regarding disallowance of certain cost-sharing expenses under the Intercompany Service Agreement with BSPA, on settlement of the dispute, the adjusted amount of ₹140.63 million was recovered from BSPA in accordance with the secondary adjustment provisions provided under the Income Tax Act, 1961.
*Total income (continuing operations)*  . Total income (continuing operations)  increased by 9.93% from ₹17,019.78 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹18,710.52 million in Fiscal 2024, primarily driven by higher revenue from operations (continuing operations).
Revenue from operations (continuing operations). Revenue from operations (continuing operations) increased by 10.46% from ₹16,875.07 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹18,640.79 million in Fiscal 2024, primarily due to an increase in total revenue from contract with customers by 9.40% from ₹16,690.92 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹18,260.26 million in Fiscal 2024, which was primarily a result of an increase in revenue from sale of goods that increased by 9.48% from ₹16,641.14 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹18,218.31 million in Fiscal 2024.
The increase in revenue from sale of goods was driven by an increase in sale of gear boxes that increased by 15.13% from ₹9,617.39 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹11,072.63 million in Fiscal 2024, primarily as a result of an increase in sales to customers in the Wind Solutions vertical. Revenue from our Wind Solutions vertical increased by 19.66% from ₹5,009.58 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹5,994.29 million in Fiscal 2024, which was largely attributable to an increase in order size/ price of an order placed by a customer in the United States.
