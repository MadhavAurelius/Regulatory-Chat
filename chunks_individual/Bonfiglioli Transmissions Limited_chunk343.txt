## * **Established parentage and trusted brand backed by global and local R&D capabilities** *

Our Company was incorporated in 1998 by Bonfiglioli S.p.A., one of the largest players in mechanical and electro- mechanical power transmissions and industrial drive solutions globally, in terms of revenue which was Euro 1,190.9 million for the year ended December 31, 2024, according to the F&S Report. We derive significant strategic advantage from the Bonfiglioli Group’s brand equity, which is globally recognized for its technical expertise and engineering excellence in mechanical and electro-mechanical power transmission and industrial automation drives (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). Leveraging our strong parentage, we have successfully developed our own brand visibility and reputation by forging direct relationships with OEMs and component vendors in India and proven after-sales service to satisfy the needs of industrial processes, automation, mobility solutions and renewable energy companies. This has also been recognized by the “Best Brand” award for 2020-2021 that was given to us by Economic Times in 2021.  We have been able to achieve this through our R&D expertise, highly diversified product offerings, and automated and digitalized manufacturing capabilities.
We extensively draw on the Bonfiglioli Group’s breadth of products and depth of global R&D capabilities to introduce new products in the Indian market. We particularly benefit from access to the Bonfiglioli Group’s India R&D Centre located in Chennai (Tamil Nadu) which is owned and operated by one of our Group Companies, BTSPL. As of September 30, 2025, the India R&D Centre had more than 100 engineers and was the only test laboratory in India to test yaw and pitch drives for wind turbines (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). The close integration of our manufacturing operations with the Group’s global R&D ecosystem enables faster product development through stronger design manufacturing alignment and early supplier engagement. This accelerates time-to-market, enhances responsiveness to India-specific customer requirements, and strengthens our positioning as a specialized solutions provider in India (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). This has also been recognized by the “Innovation of the Year” award that was given to us by Equipment India in 2025.
