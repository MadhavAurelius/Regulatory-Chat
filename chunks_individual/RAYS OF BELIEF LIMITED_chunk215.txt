#### **Employee Stock Option Scheme**
The Moms belief India Equity Appreciation Rights -  Class E Plan, 2022 (  *“*   **Plan**   *”*  ) was adopted by the Company pursuant to a Board Resolution dated July 19, 2022, and approved by the shareholders through an Ordinary Resolution on the same date.
Subsequently, the Company rescinded the said Plan pursuant to a Board Resolution dated August 01, 2025, and a Special Resolution dated August 05, 2025. The current employees have provided their consent to such rescission by way of a waiver and consent letter  **.**  As a result, all vested Equity Appreciation Rights held by eligible individuals have been forfeited, and all unvested Equity Appreciation Rights are deemed null and void, having no further effect.
