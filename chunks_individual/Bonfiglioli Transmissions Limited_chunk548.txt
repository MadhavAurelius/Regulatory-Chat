We cater to a wide range of industries under our Industry & Automation vertical with no single industry contributing more than 10% of our revenue under the Industry & Automation vertical in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and in Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022. The Industry & Automation vertical benefits from our strong and loyal dealer network which, as of September 30, 2025 comprised of 44 dealers of which 16 dealers had been engaged with us for 20 years or more.
