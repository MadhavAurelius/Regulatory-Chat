## **10. Key Audit Matters**

#### **Acquisition of Subsidiary and Recognition of Provisional Goodwill**

The fair values of the assets acquired and liabilities assumed have been determined on a provisional basis pending the finalization of a detailed independent valuation report. In accordance with Ind AS 103, any adjustments to these provisional values resulting from new information obtained about facts and circumstances that existed at the acquisition date will be recognized retrospectively within the measurement period, which will not exceed one year from the acquisition date (i.e., by June 23, 2026)." The Company has acquired 100% of Mom’s Belief US, Inc. for a total consideration of $1.5 million. The purchase price was structured as follows:

- $0.30 millions in cash paid at closing

- $0.30 millions in Buyer equity shares (Rollover Component)

- $0.90 millions in stock options, as per below details:

 a) $0.165 millions in Time-Linked Options (0.41% equity) b) $0.735 millions in Performance-Linked Options, subject to EBITDA targets over 3 years
As at the reporting date, the stock options, time-linked and performance-linked stock options have not been granted. Accordingly, the fair value assessment of such option-based consideration, including determination of whether the same constitutes contingent consideration or share-based payment, is provisional and subject to final evaluation under Ind AS 103
The Goodwill of INR 62.71 million recognized upon the acquisition of Mom's Belief US Inc as on 23 June 202025. primarily represents:
a) Expected Synergies: The operational efficiencies arising from integrating the US-based allergy and immunology practices with the Group's existing neurodevelopmental therapy protocols, allowing for cross-disciplinary care pathways. b) Assembled Workforce: The value of the specialized medical and administrative team in the US, which does not qualify for separate recognition as an intangible asset under Ind AS 103. c) Strategic Market Entry: The premium paid to gain immediate access to the US healthcare market and regulatory licenses.
