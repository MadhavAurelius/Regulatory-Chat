## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Phase 3: Pre-Launch Preparation** *

The final stage before a new centre goes live is the pre-launch preparation phase, which ensures the centre is not only physically ready but also clinically operational and known to the community it will serve. This phase involves efforts across recruitment, training, marketing, and operational readiness.
The first step is clinical and administrative staffing. The recruitment process is centrally managed, focusing on sourcing qualified therapists, psychologists, special educators, and centre heads from within the cluster geography to ensure long-term retention and cultural fit. In parallel, administrative staff and support roles are filled, creating a complete, functional unit ready to execute our care model from day one. Once the team is in place, our clinical operations team initiates a structured induction program, which includes shadowing by experienced team members. New hires are also supported through supervision and random session audits to ensure fluency in our multidisciplinary, parent-empowered care approach. On the completion of 15 days, the assistant manager of the centre, conducts an evaluation by doing an internal quality check and by calling parents for feedback on the new hire.
In tandem with team readiness, we utilize digital outreach tools such as google, meta, and local parenting networks to ensure visibility amongst our target clients. This outreach helps set up a referral network with local paediatricians, neurologists, and playschools as well. On-ground branding such as centre signage, standees, and posters are also set up at this stage. Additionally, community introduction events and opening events are held which are often in collaboration with local doctors. These launch activities are instrumental in generating early leads, gathering community feedback, and building momentum before the centres officially go live.

*“Our New Centre Roll-Out Process”*
Only upon satisfactory completion of these phases, is the final clearance issued for centre inauguration. This process helps to ensure that every centre opens with operational and clinical strength and community alignment  **.**
