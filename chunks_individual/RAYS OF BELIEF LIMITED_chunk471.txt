## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Our Comprehensive Five-Phase Care Model** *

We ensure that every family embarks on a structured, five-phase care journey that ensures personalized, outcome- oriented, and continuous support.
*Phase 1: Initial Consultation*
The journey begins with a consultation session, either in-person or virtually, through our digital services, with qualified clinical professionals. This step serves as the family’s first point of contact with us and is critical for establishing trust and clarity.
During this stage, we:
i.  Understand parental concerns, observations, and the child’s developmental history; ii.  Identify any red flags across domains such as communication, behaviour, cognition, or motor development; and iii.  Offer guidance on the next logical steps, such as screening tools, assessments, or therapy initiation.
The consultation also introduces families to our philosophy of parent empowerment and the structure of care they can expect.
*Phase 2: Comprehensive Assessment*
Post the consultation, we conduct a comprehensive developmental assessment using standard assessment tools. Assessments are tailored to the child's age, condition, and behaviour.
Some of the standard assessment tools we use include: *i.*   Pearson Clinical Assessments *ii.*   Childhood Autism Rating Scale (CARS) *iii.*   Wechsler Intelligence Scales *iv.*   VB-MAPP (Verbal Behaviour Milestones Assessment and Placement Program) *v.*   Developmental Profile 3 (DP-3)  *(Source: CARE Report, on page 63)*
Thereafter, our clinical professionals issue a neurodevelopmental profile report, highlighting the child's strengths, challenges, and a clinical interpretation of the findings, which becomes the foundation for goal planning.
*Phase 3: Enrolment and Goal Planning*
Once assessments are complete, we onboard the family into the appropriate therapy program or support model, such as in-centre therapy, digital sessions, or the family support program, which is an e-therapy program provided through the digital mode.
