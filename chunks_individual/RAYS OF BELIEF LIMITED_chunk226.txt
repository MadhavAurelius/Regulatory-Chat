## **7. Shareholding pattern of our Company**

#### * **Details of the utilisation of Net Proceeds** *

Our Board by way of its resolution dated August 08, 2025, has approved the proposal for capital expenditure for the New Centres in the next three Fiscals.
Our Company operates through a diversified network of centres, each designed to address specific needs within the neurodevelopmental care ecosystem. The  *Company Learning Centres*  are wholly established and managed by the Company, ensuring complete control over service quality, operations, and client experience. In addition,  *Company* *Learning Centres in partnership with Licensed Professionals*  combine the Company’s expertise with the credibility and reach of certified practitioners, enabling a wider footprint and deeper community integration. Through  *School* *Collaboration Centres*  , we embed our services within existing educational institutions, creating inclusive environments and early intervention opportunities for children in mainstream settings. The  *Centres for Excellence and* *Research*  (COER) serve as hubs for advancing knowledge, innovation, and best practices in the domain of neurodevelopmental disorders, bridging the gap between research and real-world application. Complementing this network is the  *Upskilling Academy*  , an in-house training platform dedicated to continuous learning, professional development, and capacity building for therapists, educators, and allied professionals, thereby strengthening the overall quality and impact of services delivered. For further details on these centres, please see  *“Our Business - Our* *Centres”*  on page 235.
In the ordinary course of our business and to build our track record of expansion, our Company has been increasing its presence across geographies in India. The number of centres of our Company has grown from 71 centres in Fiscal 2023 to 111 centres in Fiscal 2025, followed by 136 centres as on September 30, 2025. We have a wider presence of our centres across India with 43 centres in Tier 1, 76 centres in Tier 2 and 17 centres in Tier 3 cities in India as on September 30, 2025.
The Company has formulated a strategic expansion plan to establish centres across more than 25 states and union territories in India. This initiative is designed to encompass a balanced portfolio of Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities.
