## **INTERNAL RISKS**

As our Promoter and significant entity of the Bonfiglioli Group, BSPA also provides corporate services to its subsidiaries, including our Company for a fee  varies between each cluster of services and is governed by an Intercompany Service Agreement dated January 1, 2024 (the “  **Intercompany Service Agreement**  ”). Under the Intercompany Service Agreement, BSPA provides certain services to our Company (among the other entities forming part of the Bonfiglioli Group) using a combination of BSPA’s own resources as well as resources available in other companies of the Bonfiglioli Group. These services relate to functions such as information technology, internal and external communication, administration and finance, legal and governance, human resources, quality and aftersales and other centralized services more connected with our business such as product marketing, sales development, operations and purchases related services, among others. The Intercompany Service Agreement has a validity of one year and is automatically renewed for successive one-year terms.
Either party can terminate the License Agreement by giving the other party a notice within three months prior to December 31 of each fiscal year, and either party can terminate the Intercompany Service Agreement by giving the other party a notice of 30 days prior to the year-end. In addition, the License Agreement may be terminated for breach of terms specified therein if such breach is not remedied within a reasonable period following written notice. Any such termination could prevent us from being able to manufacture and sell our products under this arrangement, which in turn will adversely impact our business and operations. If any material adverse change occurs in our relationship with BSPA, or in BSPA’s business, or if BSPA ceases to provide its inputs for our business operations, our business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows may be adversely affected.
