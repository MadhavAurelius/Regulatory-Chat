The Competition Act, 2002, as amended (the “  **Competition Act**  ”) was enacted for the purpose of preventing practices that have or are likely to have an appreciable adverse effect on competition (“  **AAEC**  ”) in relevant markets in India and has mandated the Competition Commission of India (the “  **CCI**  ”) to address such practices. Under the Competition Act, any arrangement, understanding or action in concert between enterprises, whether formal or informal, which causes or is likely to cause an AAEC is deemed void and attracts substantial penalties.
