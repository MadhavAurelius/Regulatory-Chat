## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **The Code on Wages, 2019 (the “Wage Code”)** *
The Code on Wages, 2019 received the assent of the President of India on August 08, 2019. The Ministry of Labour and Employment vide notification dated December 18, 2020, notified certain provisions of the Wage Code. Through its notification dated November 21, 2025, the GoI brought into force the remaining provisions of this code. It subsumes four existing laws namely, the Payment of Wages Act, 1936, the Minimum Wages Act, 1948, the Payment of Bonus Act, 1965 and the Equal Remuneration Act, 1976.
