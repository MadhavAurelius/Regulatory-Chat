## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Research and Development (“R&D”) focused approach and digital adaptability** *

*Research-informed social enterprise:*
Our programs and plans, especially the Family Support Program, are prepared using evidence-based practices to ensure that our programs are effective and hence, are based on researched studies such as those focused on family and parent mediated training and care provided to children covering established studies and techniques such as the preschool autism communication trial (“  **PACT**  ”) and joint attention, symbolic play, engagement, and regulation
(“  **JASPER**  ”), which are disclosed on our website. We have also published two research reports in an international journal, also disclosed on our website. We are focused on continuously building and refining our programmes to align with global best practices. Our internal R&D function develops tools such as child progress tracking frameworks, parent guidance modules, and developmental play-based activities, which are designed to personalize therapeutic and developmental support and enhance outcome measurability. We have a team of eight employees dedicated towards our R&D efforts.
*Leveraging digital evolution to provide our services:*
We have integrated technology in our services and provide e-therapy programs through digital mode to our clients. Our therapy and care models such as the family support program are held virtually, through digital systems. During the COVID-19 pandemic we were able to provide our services through virtual sessions to children across 13 countries other than India.
