## **2. Majicl Healthcare Private Limited**

#### **Eligibility for the Issue**

-  Target Population/Beneficiaries: The Company focuses on children with autism, ADHD, intellectual disabilities,

 and other developmental delays, who are among the most underserved in terms of healthcare and educational resources in India.
-  Accessibility to Underserved Regions: Services are extended beyond major metropolitan cities to tier-2, tier-3

 cities, where specialised mental healthcare facilities are scarce.
-  Affordability & Inclusion: Programs are designed to be affordable and accessible, with discounts to economical

 weaker sections, remote guidance for families unable to afford full-time clinical care.
-  Capacity Building for Families: The Company empowers parents and caregivers in underserved communities

 through training, workshops, and counselling, enabling them to provide ongoing support to the child.
The Company is in compliance with requirements with Regulation 292E (2) (c) (ii) of SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018 to be identified as “For Profit Social Enterprise”.
