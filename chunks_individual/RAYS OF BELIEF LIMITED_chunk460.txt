## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **We offer comprehensive, multidisciplinary, high-quality care and follow a client-focused approach** *

The in-depth experience and expertise of our clinical team helps us prepare IEPs and IGPs which specifically target the requirements of each child, helping us provide the therapy that is best suited for the child’s development.
*Delivering value to all our key stakeholders:*
Our approach emphasizes both preventive and interventional aspects of child development. While our plans and programs are designed to  enable children with NDDs to develop essential skills and enhance their quality of life, we recognize that caregivers play a central role in reinforcing therapeutic and overall developmental outcomes of children with NDDs and accordingly our parental training programmes such as the parental guidance and family support programs focus on parental empowerment by offering required skills and knowledge to help parents in aspects of emotional intelligence, empathy, stress management, and values-based parenting.
Additionally, our collaborations with Licensed Professionals and schools not only help in guiding children in need of our services to us, but also in raising awareness on NDDs, by way of the workshops and training sessions conducted. These workshops and training sessions are not limited to just families of children with NDDs, we also engage in providing continuous training to our clinical professionals through such forums. We also actively conduct continuing medical education (“  **CME**  ”) programs for paediatricians and child health professionals.
