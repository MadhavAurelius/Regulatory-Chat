## **INTERNAL RISKS**

In India, we face competition from domestic gearbox and drive systems manufacturers across multiple industrial segments. Competition has intensified in recent years amidst competitively priced, technologically advanced product launches by all players as well as recent entrants. According to the F&S Report, our competitors in India for I&A customers include SEW-Eurodrive India Private Limited, Elecon Engineering Company Limited, NORD Drive Systems Private Limited, Premium Transmission Limited, Shanthi Gears Limited and Flender Drives Private Limited; for the Off-Highway Mobility Industry include SUNGBO Industrial Co., DANA India Private Limited, Comer Industries India Private Limited and PMP Drive Systems India Private Limited; and for the Wind Industry include ZF Wind Power Coimbatore Private Limited and Liebherr India Private Limited. We compete based on the following factors:
-  ability to accurately identify and respond to emerging technological, regulatory and industry trends and

 consumer demands for product features and performance characteristics;
-  product performance, quality and reliability;
-  successful and timely development of new and innovative products and manufacturing processes;
-  competitive pricing;
-  effectiveness of manufacturing and sales and marketing efforts;
-  successful and timely expansion of production capacity;
-  ability to provide a stable supply of products in a timely manner to our trade partners and consumers;

 and
-  brand recognition and financial strength.
While we believe we have competed effectively in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and in Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022, our existing and future competitors may have significantly greater financial resources and technical capabilities, manufacturing scale, distribution networks and brand recognition that is comparable to, or more developed than, our own. In addition, we compete with local manufacturers in our export markets that may have greater familiarity and experience in dealing in such markets. If competitors’ solutions surpass ours in terms of quality, performance, technological advancement, or pricing, our market share, profitability, business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows may be materially and adversely affected. If competitors consolidate or enter strategic partnerships, they may achieve greater economies of scale and faster time-to-market. Any failure by us to develop new technologies or react to changes in existing technologies such as electric drivetrains, smart manufacturing systems, or IoT enabled solutions, could delay our product development, resulting in loss of competitiveness, decreased revenue and market share loss. Additionally, increased competition could lower unit sales volumes and create downward pricing pressure, adversely affecting our business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows. Since our business focuses on mechanical gearbox and drive systems, adoption of alternative technologies such as direct drive systems or technologies that bypass gearbox requirements may lead to declining demand for our products, which may have a material adverse effect on our business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows.
