## **Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) Initiatives**
We are an ESG-focused manufacturing company and have invested in transitioning to a sustainable enterprise. Initiatives we have taken include integration of fume extraction systems in machining areas to reduce workplace air pollutants, renovated our scrap yard to improve waste segregation and storage to minimize land contamination risks, implemented spill pallets for oil and chemical storage areas to minimize environmental damage from accidental oil spills, adopted water-based paints for all painting operations to reduce use of hazardous chemicals, and decreased use of domestic timber by adopting alternative packaging materials.
We also operate two projects in India, CheerFutureLand and CheerFutureRoseLand, providing safe homes and educational opportunities for orphaned and disadvantaged children. Both facilities aim to offer nurturing environments and a range of recreational and vocational activities. Our support for these initiatives reflects our longstanding firm values and commitment to positive social impact.
