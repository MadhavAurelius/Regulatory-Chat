The list of our KPIs along with brief explanation of the relevance of the KPI for our business operations are set forth below:
**KPI Explanation** Revenue from Operations Revenue from operations is used by our management to track the overall financial performance of our Company and size and scale of our business Revenue from Customers in India % Share of revenue from customers in India indicates the size and scale of our business within India, and helps assess the performance of domestic business Revenue from Customers Outside India %
Share of Revenue from customers outside India reflects the size and scale of our overseas operations Revenues from Verticals Revenue from verticals is used to assess the profile of our business and track performance of each vertical Industry & Automation % Off Highway Mobility Industry % Wind Industry % Others % EBITDA (excluding Other Income) EBITDA (excluding Other Income) is crucial because it is a key performance indicator that is reflection of our Company’s profitability before interest, depreciation, amortisation and taxes EBITDA (excluding Other Income) Margin (%)
EBITDA (excluding Other Income) Margin is an indicator of the operational profitability and financial performance of our business Profit after tax for the period/year Provides information regarding the profitability of the business carried on by our Company PAT Margin (%) PAT Margin reflects the overall profitability of the business of our Company Net Debt Net Debt helps to track our leverage position and profile adjusted for cash. Cash Conversion Cycle Cash Conversion Cycle measures how quickly our Company turns its investments in inventory and receivables into cash through customer payments, indicating the working capital efficiency of the business.
