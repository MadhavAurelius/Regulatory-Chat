## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Monitoring our centre operations** *

We maintain operational standards across our network of centres, which are central to ensuring consistent, quality care. Accordingly, we have implemented a multi-tiered monitoring framework, built on Standard Operating Procedures (“  **SOPs**  ”) that guide everything from therapy delivery and parent engagement to centre maintenance and administrative workflows. Our centre coordinator SOP governs day-to-day operations, covering lead management, scheduling, fee tracking, and data reporting through employed tools. Coordinators also act as the first point of contact for parents, ensuring a seamless and supportive experience from inquiry to ongoing therapy. Regular reconciliations and MTD (“  **Month-To-Date**  ”) reporting cycles ensure real-time monitoring and accountability, while escalation mechanisms ensure swift resolution of any gaps.
Operational quality is further reinforced through our quality assurance SOP, which includes a structured parent feedback system. Anonymous surveys, direct interactions, and structured review protocols allow us to monitor satisfaction across therapy quality, staff behaviour, child progress, and hygiene. All feedback is reviewed weekly, and concerns are escalated to relevant departments for timely resolution. Positive feedback is recognized and shared internally to promote a culture of continuous improvement.
