## **Amendments to our Memorandum of Association in the last 10 years**

Set out below are the amendments to our Memorandum of Association of our Company in the 10 years preceding the date of this Draft Red Herring Prospectus:
**Date of Shareholders’ resolution/Effective date**
**Details of amendment**
November 10, 2017
Clause V of the Memorandum of Association of our Company was amended to reflect the increase in the authorized share capital of our Company from ₹ 1,250,000,000 divided into 125,000,000 Equity Shares of face value of ₹ 10 each to ₹ 1,282,000,000 divided into 128,200,000 Equity Shares of face value of ₹ 10 each. December 15, 2021 Clause V of the Memorandum of Association of our Company was amended to reflect the increase in the authorized share capital of our Company from ₹ 1,282,000,000 divided into 128,200,000 Equity Shares of face value of ₹ 10 each to ₹ 1,320,000,000 divided into 132,000,000 Equity Shares of face value of ₹ 10 each. September 1, 2025 Clause I of the Memorandum of Association of our Company was substituted to reflect the change in the name of our Company from ‘Bonfiglioli Transmissions Private Limited’ to ‘Bonfiglioli Transmissions Limited’ consequent to the conversion of our Company from a private limited company to a public limited company. November 10, 2025 Clause V of the Memorandum of Association of our Company was amended to reflect the increase in the authorized share capital of our Company from ₹ 1,320,000,000 divided into 132,000,000 Equity Shares of face value of ₹ 10 each to ₹ 1,879,958,340 divided into 187,995,834 Equity Shares of face value of ₹ 10 each.
