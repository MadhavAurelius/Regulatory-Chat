## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### **Our Competitive Strengths**

The decision to launch a new centre in a particular zone is typically based on demand indicators such as paediatric and neurological referrals, accessibility, and caregiver reach. This model allows us to:
-  optimize manpower deployment,

-  streamline operational oversight, and

-  reduce the cost of business development through shared resources amongst the centres.
All our centres operate on leased premises and schools we collaborate with, due to which we have reduced upfront investment expenditure.  The Licensed Professionals we partner up with, refer children who they deem to need our intervention plans, while partner schools help identify such students and refer them to our centres set up in their school. These models help us build a wide base of clients.
We have built our brand over the last seven years, since the commencement of our operations. We have received awards that recognize our brand as pre-eminent. For example, we were recognized as one of the most valued mother and child brands by the Times of India in 2020. We were also awarded the Innovative Practice Award, 2019, awarded by the Zero Project to our brand, Mom’s Belief, at the United Nations, Vienna, Austria.
