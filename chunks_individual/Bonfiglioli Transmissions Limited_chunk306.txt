Yaw drives form the larger share of India’s wind-drive value pool. Pitch drives account for a smaller but essential share, supported by the steady move toward advanced blade-control technologies. Yaw drives show the fastest scale-up, nearly doubling by Fiscal 2030. Pitch drives also rise significantly with improved CUF targets and OEM upgrades, signalling a broader technology upgrade cycle.
Bonfiglioli is among the earliest companies to establish domestic manufacturing of wind-turbine pitch and yaw drives in India, beginning in 2006 at its Chennai (Tamil Nadu) facility. In line with India’s market growth, Bonfiglioli expanded its drive capacities and became the first in the country to manufacture pitch drives for 1- 5 MW wind turbines.
