### **D. HOW WILL THE INVESTMENT STRATEGY BENCHMARK ITS PERFORMANCE?**

The performance of the Investment Strategy will be benchmarked to the performance of  **NIFTY 50 Hybrid Composite Debt 50:50 Index.**
**Rationale for adoption of benchmark:-** The composition of the benchmark is such that it is most suited for comparing performance of the investment strategy.
The fund reserves the right to change the said benchmark and/or adopt one/more other benchmarks to compare the performance of the Investment strategy. The performance of this investment strategy will be compared with its peers in the industry. The performance will be placed before the Investment Committee as well as the Board of Directors of the AMC and the Trustee Company in each of their meetings.
