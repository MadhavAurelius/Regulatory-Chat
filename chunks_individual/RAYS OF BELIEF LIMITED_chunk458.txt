## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Focus on accessibility** *

*Accessibility by spreading awareness through outreach programs and collaborations:*
We are focused on creating awareness about NDDs and fostering community involvement, which is crucial for early identification, appropriate support and reducing stigma. Our initiatives include advocacy, social media campaigns and community engagement.
We conduct community awareness and engagement programs for disseminating relevant information and guidance to affected families and caregivers. For further details, please see “  *Our Business - Key Outreach Pillars*  ” on page 246.
Our outreach is also done through our digital channels, where we deliver live webinars, and informative content to ensure that early-identification messaging reaches families across all socioeconomic strata. These are delivered through various channels including a portal on our website under the name Mom’s Belief Community. Through this portal, parents and caregivers of children with NDDs get to connect with each other and share their experiences. Free counselling and advice is also provided by our clinical professionals. Along with the website, we have Whatsapp groups with parents to allow for co-ordination and communication amongst parents.
A core component of our efforts to ensure accessibility involves working closely with community health systems. We have initiated large-scale training programs for frontline workers, particularly ASHA and ANM workers, to strengthen early identification of neurodivergent children. For further details, please see “  *Our Business*  -  *Environmental, Social* *and Governance*  ” on page 248.
