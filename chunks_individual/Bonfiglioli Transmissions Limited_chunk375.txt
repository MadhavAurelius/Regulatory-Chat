## **Information Technology: Data Security and Protection**
Our IT operations are supported by the Global Digital Services (“  **GDS**  ”) team which is housed in BTSPL, one of our Group Companies. The GDS team manages and supports essential systems, including SAP platforms for finance, supply chain, and business management, plus tools for warehouse management, productivity, and customer engagement. It also oversees system integration and business intelligence solutions, which help connect data sources and provide actionable insights for the organization.
The GDS team also maintains the infrastructure that powers and protects our technological environment. This includes cybersecurity measures, network security, data center and cloud solutions for reliability, and support for everyday collaboration.
