## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Environmental, Social and Governance** *

*Environment*
Although we operate primarily in the healthcare and education services sector, where the environmental footprint is relatively limited compared to industrial sectors, we remain mindful of our ecological responsibilities. Across our centres and operations, we have adopted practices such as digital-first communication.
Our therapy kits and resource materials are mainly made of cardboard, with minimal plastic usage. Going forward, we plan to integrate green procurement guidelines, energy tracking systems, and localised waste segregation at larger centres, reinforcing our commitment to environmental sustainability in every dimension of our growth.
*Social*
A core component of our efforts as a social enterprise is to ensure accessibility involves working closely with community health systems. We have initiated large-scale training programs for frontline workers, particularly ASHA and ANM workers, to strengthen early identification of neurodivergent children. We equip these grassroots workers
with the tools to identify early signs of autism, ADHD, and developmental delays, and to educate families about the importance of early intervention. We also equip the workers with the necessary information to assist families in accessing available benefits, by also informing them about relevant government support systems such as the unique disability ID (“  **UDID**  ”) and the Niramaya Health Insurance Scheme.
By collaborating with ASHA and ANM, we have delivered impactful training sessions in regions such as Pune, Gurgaon, and Delhi. These programs are not only building community-level awareness but also laying the foundation for a sustainable referral and support system. This initiative demonstrates our vision of inclusive healthcare one that begins at the grassroots and creates long-term, system-level change.
*Governance*
Strong governance forms the backbone of our growth and credibility. We are committed to maintaining transparency, accountability, and ethical practices in all aspects of our business. Our Company operates under a clearly defined legal and corporate structure, with board-level oversight, audited financials, and adherence to all applicable statutory regulations. We have established internal policies on data protection, employee conduct, grievance redressal, anti- discrimination, and conflict of interest, all of which are regularly reviewed by our leadership. Our digital systems are protected by industry-grade information security protocols and managed through role-based access controls, audit trails, and compliance monitoring. Our leadership team is committed to upholding principles of fairness, diversity, and stakeholder inclusion.
