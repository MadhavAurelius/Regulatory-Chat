**CONSIDERATION OF ISSUES AND EVIDENCE** 13. I have perused the allegations levelled against the Noticee in the SCN and the material available on record. In the instant matter, the following issues arise for consideration and determination: I. Whether the Noticee has violated regulations 3(a), (b), (c), (d) and 4(1) and 4(2)(a) of PFUTP Regulations? II. Do the violations, if any, on the part of the Noticee attract monetary penalty under section 15HA of SEBI Act? III. If so, what would be the quantum of monetary penalty that can be imposed on the Noticee after taking into account the factors mentioned in section 15J of the SEBI Act?
