## **Senior Management**

**Naresh Rajendran**  is the Director of Human Resources in our Company and he is responsible for overseeing human resources and employee relations of our Company. He has been associated with our Company since December 2025. He holds a degree in bachelor’s of business administration from the University of Madras and a master’s of social work from the University of Madras. He was previously associated with Grundfos Pumps India Private Limited, Vestas Wind Technology India Private Limited, and HCL Technologies Limited. He has over 21 years of experience in human resources management. In Fiscal 2025, he received an aggregate compensation of ₹ 0.39 million from our Company  and no compensation has been accrued for Fiscal 2025 which is payable at a later date.
