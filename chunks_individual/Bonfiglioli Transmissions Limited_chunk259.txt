## **India Industrial Gearboxes Market: Segmentation by End-user Markets**

India Industrial Gearboxes, End-user Contribution, Fiscal 2025

The Indian industrial gearbox market, valued at approximately ₹ 38,000 million, remains heavily concentrated in core process industries such as cement, steel, and sugar, which together account for a dominant share of overall demand. Notably, highest-growth momentum is visible in defense and ports & shipbuilding, where gearboxes are being adopted for heavy-duty cranes, marine drives, and propulsion systems. These require precision, compact design, and reliability in harsh environments, favoring planetary and bevel-helical configurations.
