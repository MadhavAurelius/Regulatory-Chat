## * **Fiscal 2023 compared to Fiscal 2022** *

*Total income (continuing operations).*  Total income (continuing operations) increased by 8.38% from ₹15,703.32 million for Fiscal 2022 to ₹17,019.78 million for Fiscal 2023, driven primarily by growth in revenue from operations.
Revenue from operations (continuing operations). Revenue from operations (continuing operations) increased by 8.26% from ₹15,588.01 million for Fiscal 2022 to ₹16,875.07 million for Fiscal 2023, primarily due to an increase in revenue from contract with customers which increased by 8.44% from ₹15,392.18 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹16,690.92 million in Fiscal 2023, predominantly due to an increase in sales of goods, which increased by 8.41% from ₹15,349.74 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹16,641.14 million in Fiscal 2023, and increase in income from services which increased by 17.30% from ₹42.44 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹49.78 million in Fiscal 2023.
The increase in revenue from operations (continuing operations) was marginally offset by a decrease in other operating revenue (continuing operations) that decreased by 5.96% from ₹195.83 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹184.15 million in Fiscal 2023, on account of a decrease in revenue from scrap sales from ₹87.62 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹78.21 million in Fiscal 2023.
Other income (continuing operations) . Other income (continuing operations) increased by 25.50% from ₹115.31 million for Fiscal 2022 to ₹144.71 million for Fiscal 2023. This increase was driven primarily by an increase in the interest income on bank deposits, which increased by 27.27% from ₹109.10 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹138.85 million in Fiscal 2023.
*Total expenses (continuing operations)*  . Total expenses (continuing operations) increased by 10.97% from ₹13,452.12 million for Fiscal 2022 to ₹14,927.44 million for Fiscal 2023, mainly due to an increase in cost of raw materials & components consumed (continuing operations), employee benefits expense (continuing operations), finance costs (continuing operations), other expenses (continuing operations).
Cost of raw materials & components consumed (continuing op er at i o n s ). Cost of raw materials & components consumed (continuing operations) increased by 12.00% from ₹9,921.60 million for Fiscal 2022 to ₹11,112.52 million for Fiscal 2023, due to increased purchases (net), which increased by 16.89% from ₹9,864.32 million for Fiscal 2022 to ₹11,530.84 million for Fiscal 2023, on account of higher consumption to meet higher sales.
