#### **INDIAN SPEECH DISORDER MARKET**
Speech disorders affect an individual's ability to produce sounds correctly or fluently, impeding effective communication. These disorders encompass a wide range of conditions, from stuttering to articulation and phonological disorders. In India, speech disorders are a growing concern, affecting children and adults alike, with increasing awareness and advancements in diagnostic and therapeutic approaches shaping the market.
