*Strong sales team, dealer network and after-sales service support*
We attribute our long-standing relationships with Industry & Automation customers to the range of products and after-sales services we offer, including through our dealer network. We have a strong dealer network that helps distribute our broad range of products to customers across industries. We usually enter into annual arrangements including auto-renewal clauses with our dealers and pay commission based on slabs determined by the number of orders booked on a quarterly and annual basis. We also provide incentives based on: (a) monthly purchase volumes; (b) quarterly sales; and (c) sales performance levelled over three quarters. As of September 30, 2025, 16 out of 44 of our dealers had been associated with us for 20 or more years, and more than 81% of our dealers engaged with us on an exclusive basis, reflecting a strong commitment to our business and the Bonfiglioli brand.
