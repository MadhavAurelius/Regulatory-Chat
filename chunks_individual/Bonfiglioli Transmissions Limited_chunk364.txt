## **Research and Development**

We benefit from access to the global research and development network of BSPA, which comprises of seven R&D centers across India, Italy, Germany and China. These R&D centers had a combined strength of 377 employees as of September 30, 2025, and undertake end-to-end product development, which includes application study, design and prototyping, simulation and validation, and product launch.
During the year ended December 31, 2024, our Company transferred a portion of its business that undertook IT related and R&D functions to a newly incorporated entity under the Bonfiglioli Group. The India R&D Center is currently located in Chennai (Tamil Nadu).
We continue to benefit from our proximity to the India R&D Center that is located in Chennai in the same region as our facilities in Mannur and Thirumudivakkam in Tamil Nadu, as it facilitates closer collaboration and efficiency across our product development process. This set-up allows for regular, direct exchanges between teams, so our engineering designs are quickly aligned with practical manufacturing capabilities from the earliest stages. We can quickly assess feasibility, address potential production challenges before they impact cost or schedules, and make optimal use of our existing equipment. It also helps us work closely with our suppliers, bringing them into the process early to ensure robust and reliable sourcing for new products.
This proximity accelerates value analysis and value engineering activities and enhances our ability to standardize processes without compromising quality. Our teams are able to develop and test prototypes, using real-time feedback and iterating swiftly to refine new concepts. These integrated ways of working significantly reduce our product development cycle and time-to-market, strengthening our competitive position and supporting long-term business growth. As of September 30, 2025, the India R&D Center was India’s only test laboratory to test yaw and pitch drives for wind turbines (  *Source: F&S Report*  ), and it develops a host of other products such as gearboxes for material handling, pharmaceutical applications, off-highway applications. It is also the only R&D center in the Bonfiglioli Group that caters to all three disciplines of mechanics, electro-mechanics and electronics at the same site.
