## **Overview**

We are one of India’s largest technology-led mechanical and electro-mechanical power transmissions and industrial drive solutions providers by revenue in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 (  *Source: F&S* *Report*  ). We manufacture a wide range of mission-critical and highly precision engineered gearboxes and drive systems used across diverse end-user markets (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). Our legacy and reputation lie in the Bonfiglioli brand, which has a worldwide presence of nearly 70 years, and which we have established in India through more than 25 years of operations.
Our Corporate Promoter, Bonfiglioli S.p.A. is headquartered in Italy, and according to the F&S Report, is one of the largest technologically advanced players globally in the mechanical and electro-mechanical power transmissions and industrial drive solutions industry with a revenue of Euro 1,190.9 million (₹105.91 billion) for the year ended December 31, 2024. It focuses on the development, manufacture and distribution of gearboxes and drive systems to cater to the needs of industrial processes, automation, off-highway mobility solutions and renewable energy applications. As at the date of this Draft Red Herring Prospectus, the Bonfiglioli Group has operations spanning across 80 countries through 17 production sites, seven R&D centres, a network of more than 550 distributors and resellers, and a workforce of more than 5,000 employees, with 21 patents granted under the name of Bonfiglioli S.p.A.
The markets we cater to are broadly categorized into three business verticals: Industry and Automation Solutions, Off-Highway Mobility and Wind Industries, based on the end-use applications. We are among the largest manufacturers in India of gearboxes and drives for original equipment manufacturers (“  **OEMs**  ”) and end-users engaged in Industry & Automation Solutions (gear motors < 15 kNm), Off-Highway Mobility and Wind Industries in India as of September 30, 2025, in terms of revenue from operations, according to the F&S Report. We were also among the leading manufacturers of planetary gearboxes in India i n the year ended December 31, 2024 by revenue.  *(Source: F&S Report)*
