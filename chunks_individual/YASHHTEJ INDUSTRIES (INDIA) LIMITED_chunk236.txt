## **5B. Regulatory and Policy Framework -  Crude Soybean Oil and DE-Oiled Cake**
The Crude Soybean Oil and De-Oiled Cake (DOC) industry in India is governed by a comprehensive regulatory framework encompassing agricultural development policies, trade regulations, environmental controls, and emerging renewable energy linkages. The government’s policy interventions seek to enhance oilseed self-reliance, support sustainable processing, and strengthen global competitiveness.
