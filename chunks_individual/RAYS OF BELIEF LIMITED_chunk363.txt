## **11. Psychotherapy and Counselling**

#### **Table 10: Description on types of ADHD**
|  ADHD Presentation Type  |  Description  |
|:---|:---|
| Predominantly Inattentive  | -  Difficulty focusing, following instructions, and staying organized . |
|  |  -  Hyperactivity is not a prominent issue.  |
|:---|:---|
| Predominantly Hyperactive- Impulsive | -  Restlessness, inability to stay seated, and impulsive behaviours dominate.  -  Attention issues may be less evident. |
