## **Fire Prevention Laws**
State Governments have enacted laws that provide for fire prevention and life safety. Such laws include provisions in relation to providing fire safety and life saving measures by occupiers of buildings, obtaining certification in relation to compliance with fire prevention and life safety measures and impose penalties for non-compliance.
