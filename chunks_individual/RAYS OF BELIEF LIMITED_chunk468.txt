## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### **Our Business Operations**
Our Company, founded in 2017, and launching its first centre in 2018, is a multi-channel early intervention platform for children with NDDs. We offer a blend of in-person therapy at our centres across India and digital services such as virtual consultations, along with home-delivered customized learning kits. This omni-channel approach supports families in a “research online and enrol offline” journey. With strategic investments in clinical innovation, content creation, and logistics, we have developed a library of over 2,000 teaching tools, empowering parents as co-therapists. Deep community engagement through training and outreach ensures personalized, budget-sensitive interventions.
