## **INTERNAL RISKS**

* **36.** *    * **Technology failures could disrupt our operations and adversely affect our business operations and** * * **financial performance.** *
IT systems are critical to our ability to manage our large manufacturing operations, including in terms of carrying out payroll processing and travel management functions, and implementing our manufacturing excellence system. If we do not allocate and effectively manage the resources necessary to build and sustain the proper IT infrastructure, we could be subject to disruptions in our key day to day operations.
We have also introduced IoT enabled software for our yaw and pitch drives. We are therefore exposed to challenges relating to the building of new IT structures which can subject us to certain errors, inefficiencies, disruptions and, in some instances, poor customer service. Our IT systems, and the systems of our third-party IT service providers may also be vulnerable to a variety of interruptions due to events beyond our control, including, but not limited to, natural disasters, terrorist attacks, telecommunications failures, computer viruses, hackers and other security issues. While we have not experienced any such technology failure that has resulted in any significant disruption in our operation in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and in Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022, there can be no assurance that such events will not occur in the future. Although we have security initiatives and disaster recovery plans in place to mitigate its risk to these vulnerabilities, such measures may not have been effectively implemented or may not be adequate to ensure that our operations are not disrupted.
* **37.** *    * **A majority of our Directors do not have prior experience of holding a directorship in a company listed** * * **on the Stock Exchanges.** *
A majority of our Directors do not have any prior experience of holding directorship in a company listed on the Stock Exchanges. Post listing of the Equity Shares, our Company and our Directors will be subject to the applicable regulatory requirements, including the requirements prescribed under SEBI Listing Regulations. Upon listing of the Equity Shares, the responsibilities of our Directors  *inter alia*  include monitoring the effectiveness of our Company’s governance practices and suggesting changes and overseeing our financial reporting systems along with the disclosures and communication made by our Company. We cannot assure you that we will be able to comply with the applicable regulatory requirements at all times. Any non-compliance with the regulatory framework, due to lack of experience or otherwise, may subject us to adverse regulatory actions, and have an impact on the listing price of our Equity Shares.
