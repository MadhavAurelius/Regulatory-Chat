## **Key Performance Indicators (“KPIs”) (under the section titled “**   * **Basis for Offer Price** *   **” on page 104)**

**KPI Definition** Cash Conversion Cycle Cash Conversion Cycle is calculated as receivable days plus inventory days minus payable days (in which receivable days is calculated as receivables divided by revenue from operations multiplied by number of days; inventory days is calculated as inventory divided by cost of goods sold multiplied by number of days; and payable days is calculated as payables divided by purchase of raw materials and components plus stock- in-trade multiplied by number of days; where cost of goods sold is the sum of cost of material consumed, change in inventory, purchases of stock-in-trade, receivables, inventory and payables taken as of end of period/year) EBITDA (excluding Other Income)
EBITDA is calculated as Profit after tax for the period / year plus total tax expense plus finance costs plus depreciation and amortization. EBITDA (excluding Other Income) = EBITDA minus Other Income EBITDA (excluding Other Income) Margin (%)
EBITDA (excluding Other Income) divided by revenue from operations
Fixed Assets Turnover Ratio Fixed asset turnover is calculated as revenue from operations divided by Fixed Assets. Fixed Assets is calculated as sum of Property, Plant and Equipment and Capital work-in- progress at the end of the period Net Debt Net Debt is calculated as the sum of current borrowings, current lease liabilities, non- current borrowings and non-current lease liabilities minus cash and cash equivalents and bank balances other than cash and cash equivalents PAT Margin (%) Profit after tax for the period/year divided by Total Income Profit after tax for the period/year
Profit after tax for the period/year represents profit for the relevant period/year
