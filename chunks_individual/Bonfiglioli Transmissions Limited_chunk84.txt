## **INTERNAL RISKS**

We depend on BSPA for the use of the “Bonfiglioli” brand, and we share the brand name with other companies within the Bonfiglioli Group. The success of our business depends on our and BSPA’s ability to develop, maintain and strengthen the “Bonfiglioli” brand. To promote the “Bonfiglioli” brand further, we may be required to evolve our marketing practices, which could result in an increase in our costs. Various factors, some of which are beyond our or BSPA’s control, are critical for maintaining and enhancing our brand. These include our or BSPA ability to increase brand awareness among existing and potential customers, adopt new technologies or adapt our systems to customer requirements or emerging industry standards, and protect the intellectual property related to our brand. If we or BSPA fail to preserve the value of the “Bonfiglioli” brand, maintain our reputation, or attract consumers to our brand through, for example, quality after-sale services to our customers, our business could be adversely impacted. Furthermore, our and BSPA’s reputation and the “Bonfiglioli” brand is vulnerable to threats that can be

In addition, any negative publicity about us, BSPA or the “Bonfiglioli” brand, whether as a result of our own or our third-party service providers’ actual or alleged conduct, complaints or negative reviews by customers, alleged misconduct, unethical business practices, safety breaches or other improper activities, or rumours relating to our or BSPA’s business, directors, officers, employees or shareholders, could harm our reputation, business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows. While we have not experienced any such instances of negative publicity in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and in Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022, any allegations of this nature, even if not proved, may lead to inquiries, investigations, or other legal actions against us which could cause us to incur significant costs to defend ourselves.
The occurrence of any of these instances could create a negative perception of our brand and impact our reputation and operations. Any negative publicity regarding our business partners that we closely cooperate with, or any regulatory inquiries or investigations and lawsuits initiated against them, may also have an impact on the “Bonfiglioli” brand and our or BSPA’s reputation, or subject us to regulatory inquiries or investigations or lawsuits. Our brand image may also be affected negatively if customers believe we are no longer able to offer high-quality gearboxes and drive systems and parts and related services that meet evolving customer demands which, in turn, would affect our sales volumes, business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows.
