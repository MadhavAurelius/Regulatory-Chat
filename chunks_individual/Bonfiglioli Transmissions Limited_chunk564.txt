# The Company has provided loan to Bonfiglioli Technology Space Private Limited (i) amounting to Rs 115.50 (December 31, 2024 - Rs 115.50; December 31, 2023 - Nil; December 31, 2022 - Nil) relating to sale of discontinued operations, which is repayable in 5 equal annual instalments at an interest rate of 8.25% p.a and (ii) cash pooling facility amounting to Rs 12.96 (December 31, 2024- Rs. 103.01; December 31, 2023 - Nil; December 31, 2022 - Nil) at an interest rate of 8.25%, repayable within a year.

## **Income**

Income consists of revenue from operations and other income.
*Revenue from operations.*  Revenue from operations comprises sale of products, income from services, and other operating revenue.
Sale of products includes sale of goods, and revenue from sale of goods is recognized at the point in time when control of the asset is transferred to the customer, generally on delivery of the goods or ex-works depending on the contractual terms.
Income from services includes income from services rendered. Revenue from services is recognised as and when the services are rendered in accordance with the specific terms of contract with the customer. When the consideration is received, before we render service to the customer, we present the consideration as a contract liability, which is then recognised as revenue once the services are rendered.
Other operating revenue comprises revenue from scrap sales, export incentives, and miscellaneous income. In Fiscal 2024, we recorded a one-time income from secondary adjustment as per transfer pricing regulations. This arose from a dispute with the income tax department regarding disallowance of certain cost-sharing expenses under the Intercompany Service Agreement with BSPA, which was subsequently settled under the Vivad se Vishwas scheme in Fiscal 2025, whereby we paid 50% of the disputed tax liability and the remaining amount was waived. Consequently, the corresponding cost-sharing expenses were reversed and reimbursed to BSPA, resulting in this one-time income recognition in Fiscal 2024.
*Other income.*  Other income primarily comprises (i) interest income on bank deposits, other deposits, and inter- company loans, (ii) net gain on sale of property, plant and equipment, (iii) allowance for doubtful debts no longer required written back, (iv) net gain on foreign currency transactions and translation, and (v) liabilities no longer required written back.
