## * **Wind Industry** *

*Case Study -  Prominent Indian Wind OEM (“Wind OEM”)*
Since the inception of the Wind OEM’s wind business, we have supplied yaw and pitch drives for their turbines. Our primary challenge to manufacture these gearboxes locally was the absence of an established supplier base and manufacturing infrastructure for planetary swing gearboxes within India. Drawing on our technical expertise, we developed strong partnerships with domestic suppliers for large-scale forgings and castings. At the same time, we invested in building critical in-house capabilities, including the gear-cutting of large pinion teeth and broaching of ring gears. This holistic approach enabled us to manufacture these drives in India, delivering solutions tailored to the Wind OEM’s requirements and strengthening our local presence.

*Customers*
Customers under our Wind Industry vertical include well known renewable energy companies such as Senvion Wind Technology Private Limited and Suzlon Energy Limited that have been doing business with us for more than 10 years.
