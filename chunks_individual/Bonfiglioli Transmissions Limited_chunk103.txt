* **42.** *    * **Our Statutory Auditor’s examination report on our restated financial statements discloses certain** * * **modifications and certain matters specified in the Companies (Audit and Auditors) Rules, 2014 and** * * **Companies Auditor’s Report Order, 2020, that were included in the reports for the audited financial** * * **statements for the nine months period ended September 30, 2025, and Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022.** *
Our Statutory Auditor’s report on the audited financial statements for Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022, under the section legal and regulatory requirements included a modification which indicated that the daily back-up of the books of account and other relevant books and papers, in electronic mode, has not been kept on servers physically located in India. In addition, our Statutory Auditor has also made certain modification in its report on the audited financial statements for Fiscal 2024 with respect to the accounting software as well as third-party accounting software used by us for maintaining our books of account. These are: (i) that the feature of recording audit trail (edit log) facility was not enabled in the accounting software for certain tables and fields and for certain changes being made when using privileged/administrative access rights; and (ii) that the third party accounting software’s feature of recording audit trail (edit log) facility, was not operated throughout the year at the application and database layer. As a result, our Statutory Auditor was unable to comment upon whether there was any instance of audit trail feature being tampered with, where enabled, throughout the year in respect of the accounting software as well as third-party accounting software.
In addition, our Statutory Auditor in the auditor’s report to the audited financial statements for Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022, issued under the Companies Auditor’s Report Order, 2020 (CARO) included a modification which indicated that there have been delays up to 60 days of tax deducted at source, 79 days in case of professional tax and 328 days of tax collected at source in Fiscal 2024, slight delays of tax deducted at source in Fiscal 2023 and slight delays of tax deducted at source, professional tax and provident fund contributions in Fiscal 2022.
