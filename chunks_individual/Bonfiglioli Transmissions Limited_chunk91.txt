## **INTERNAL RISKS**

* **25.** *    * **Our manufacturing operations may be subject to strikes, work stoppages or increased wage demands** * * **by our employees or the employees of our sub-contractors.** *
As of September 30, 2025, we had a workforce of 975 personnel (inclusive of 590 on-roll employees and 385 contract labourers), of which 451 employees were directly engaged by us at our manufacturing sites and 375 employees were engaged as contract labour to carry out our manufacturing operations. The success of our operations depends on the availability of labour and maintaining good relationships with our workforce. Shortage of skilled and unskilled personnel or work stoppages caused by disagreements with employees. This could result in strikes, work stoppages or increased wage demands and could materially and adversely affect our business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows.
India has stringent labour legislations that protect the interests of workers. We are also subject to laws and regulations governing relationships with employees, in areas such as minimum wage and maximum working hours, overtime, working conditions, hiring and terminating of employees and work permits. See “  * **Key** * * **Regulations and Policies in India** *  ” on page 219. As of the date of this Draft Red Herring Prospectus, employees at our manufacturing facility at Thirumudivakkam, Tamil Nadu are organised in a union, and we have entered into a 3.5 year wage settlement agreement with effect from October 2025 that prescribes fixed wage increments, bonus payments, leave and holiday entitlements and disciplinary protocols. If our remaining employees similarly unionize, it may become difficult for us to maintain flexible labour policies.
While we have not experienced any disruption in our business operations due to disputes with our workforce in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and in Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022 there can be no assurance that we will not experience disruption in the future. Such disruptions may adversely affect our business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows and may also divert our management’s attention and result in increased costs.
* **26.** *    * **We may be unable to adequately protect our intellectual property rights and those of the Bonfiglioli** * * **Group and an infringement of such intellectual property may substantially harm our business.** *
Our Company does not hold any registered trademarks or patents or designs in its own name and our Company has a non-exclusive, non-transferable right and license to use intellectual property rights of one of our Corporate Promoters, Bonfiglioli S.p.A., including trademarks, patents, and other intellectual property. There can be no assurance that trademarks and patents under our pending applications will be granted or that we will be able to renew our registered trademarks and patents in a timely manner or at all. Any unauthorised or inappropriate use of the Bonfiglioli brand, trademarks and other related intellectual property rights by others in their corporate names, product brands or otherwise could harm our brand image, competitive advantages and business, and dilute or harm our reputation and brand recognition.
