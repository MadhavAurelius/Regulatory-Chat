## * **Nine months period ended September 30, 2025** *

*Total income*  . Total income was ₹14,679.54 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025, primarily comprising revenue from operations of ₹14,601.74 million and other income of ₹77.80 million.
Revenue from operations . Revenue from operations was ₹14,601.74 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025. This comprised (i) revenue from contracts with customers of ₹14,447.93 million which includes revenue from sale of goods of ₹14,398.84 million and revenue from income from services of ₹49.09 million, and (ii) other operating revenue of ₹153.81 million, which includes scrap sales of ₹62.20 million and income from government grant of ₹91.61 million.
A majority of our revenue from contracts with customers was attributable to the sale of gear motors and gear boxes that were sold under the I&A vertical and Wind Industry vertical that represented 40.98% and 31.97% of our revenue from operations in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025, respectively.
Other income. Other income was ₹77.80 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025. This comprised interest income on bank deposits of ₹51.32 million, interest income on other deposits of ₹1.74 million, interest income on loan given to related party of ₹7.83 million, gain on disposal of property, plant and equipment of ₹1.12 million, allowance for doubtful debts no longer required written back of ₹9.50 million and gain on termination of lease of ₹6.29 million.
Total expenses. Total expenses were ₹12,461.07 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025. This comprised primarily of cost of raw materials & components consumed, employee benefits expense, and other expenses.
Cost of raw materials & components consumed. Cost of raw materials and components consumed was ₹ 9,107.44 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025. This included inventory as at the beginning of the period of ₹1,643.48 million, purchases (net) of ₹9,461.80 million, and inventory as at the end of the period of ₹1,997.84 million.
