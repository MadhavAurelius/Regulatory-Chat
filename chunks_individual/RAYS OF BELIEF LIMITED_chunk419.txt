#### **Table 18: Educational Regulations and Policies**

|  Inclusive Education  |  The RPWD Act (2016) mandates inclusive education for children with disabilities, including those  with NDDs. It ensures accessible educational facilities and support, like special educators or therapy  services.  |
|:---|:---|
| Disability  Certification  | Disability certification in India involves assessments by specialists in neurodevelopmental disorders.  This certification is essential for accessing government schemes and educational benefits.  |
