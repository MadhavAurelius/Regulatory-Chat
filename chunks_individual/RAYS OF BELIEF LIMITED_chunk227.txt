In the last three fiscal years, the Company has closed certain centres as part of a strategic decision to discontinue the "Network Centres" business model. This model, launched in Fiscal 2019, involved partnerships with existing therapy centres. It was discontinued to consolidate operations under a more standardized and centrally governed structure, ensuring consistent quality of care across our network. Total cost incurred for the 45 Centres closed during the last 3 years was ₹ 0.42 million.
