## **10. Supportive Therapies for Specific Disorders**
-   **Cerebral Palsy**  : In addition to physical and occupational therapy, children with CP may

 benefit from speech therapy, botulinum toxin injections for muscle spasticity, and surgical interventions in severe cases.

-   **Down Syndrome**  : Early intervention programs focusing on speech, motor, and cognitive skills

 are crucial, as well as regular health monitoring for associated medical conditions (e.g., heart defects).

-   **Intellectual Disabilities**  : A combination of specialized education, life skills training, and

 community support can help individuals develop to their fullest potential.
