## * **Continue to strengthen market access and customer relationships while remaining cost effective** *
We seek to continue strengthening market access and deepening customer relationships, while maintaining a sharp focus on cost effectiveness. We propose to achieve this by prioritising high-growth business verticals, particularly agriculture and concrete equipment sectors, and target rapidly growing OEMs as potential customers. We also plan to acquire new customers and increase our wallet share with existing customers by cross-selling our current broad product portfolio. Our approach to cost leadership is anchored in modular product design and ongoing improvements in supply chain efficiency, in an effort to remain competitive as we expand our presence in these key markets. As of September 30, 2025, we operated through a pan-India sales network that comprised 13 sales offices and 64 sales employees, that we propose to leverage to increase wallet share among our existing customers and acquire new customers through ongoing product development efforts.
