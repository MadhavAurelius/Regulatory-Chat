## **INTERNAL RISKS**

Continued or significant fluctuations in the cost of commodities, supply interruptions or shortages could cause our suppliers to increase their costs, which in turn may have an adverse impact on our ability to manufacture our gearboxes in line with targeted costs. While we seek to pass on the increase in cost of raw materials and components to our customers, an inability to do so may adversely impact our business, financial condition, results of operations, cash flows and lead to lower margins.
Although we source majority of our material consumed from suppliers based in India, we typically import parts from global suppliers in situations where parts and materials are not available in India, or available parts do not meet required quality standards, or are available at comparable costs, such as electric motors from Vietnam for our yaw and pitch drives that are imported at a concessional customs duty, and hydraulic motors from Japan that are imported free of duty. We additionally import bearings from suppliers in China, and components such as worm wheels, fan and box covers, rectifiers, brake rings and discs from our Corporate Promoter BSPA in Italy. We therefore depend on the economic and political conditions of these countries and geopolitical relations between these countries and India.
