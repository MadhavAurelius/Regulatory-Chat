## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Our Centre Designs** *
We follow a modular and adaptive approach to designing our centres while optimizing available space based on location-specific factors. Despite layout variations, each centre is consistently structured to include core clinical and operational zones such as assessment rooms, therapy rooms for ABA, speech and occupational therapy, parent consultation spaces, and welcoming waiting areas amongst other designated zones. Play-based engagement spaces and sensory zones are integrated wherever feasible, promoting a calm and inclusive environment for children. Each setup meets our internal standards for safety, accessibility, and regulatory compliance, including adherence to fire norms and hygiene protocols.
