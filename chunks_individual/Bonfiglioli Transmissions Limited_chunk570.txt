## * **Nine months period ended September 30, 2025** *

Changes in inventories of finished goods and work-in-progress. Inventories of finished goods and work-in- progress increased by ₹104.64 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025, due to a decrease in stock of finished goods and work-in-progress to ₹1,358.88 million over the nine months period ended September 30, 2025.
Materials Cost (continuing operations). Materials Cost (c ontinuing operations ) was ₹ 9,212.08 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025, and represented 63.09% of our revenue from operations (c ontinuing operations ) in such period.
Employee benefits expense . Employee benefits expense was ₹750.21 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025. This included salaries, wages and bonus of ₹651.76 million, contribution to provident fund of ₹36.78 million and to gratuity of ₹21.07 million, and staff welfare expenses of ₹40.60 million.
Finance costs. Finance costs were ₹52.96 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025. These comprised interest on lease liabilities of ₹2.78 million and other borrowing costs of ₹50.18 million.

Depreciation and amortisation expense . Depreciation and amortisation expense was ₹341.14 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025. This mainly comprised depreciation of property, plant and equipment of ₹309.49 million, amortisation of intangible assets of ₹1.27 million and depreciation of right-of-use asset of ₹30.38 million.
Other expenses. Other expenses were ₹2,104.68 million in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025, and primarily comprised:
-  Royalty of ₹656.02 million that were made to the BSPA in exchange for licensing the use of certain

 intellectual property rights to us for manufacture of gear box and gear motor.
-  Professional fees of ₹354.02 million incurred towards cost sharing expenses from BSPA and other

 consultancy fees paid for industrial and corporate consultancy.
-  Sub-contracting charges of ₹262.77 million incurred towards processing of raw materials.
-  Power and fuel expenses of ₹164.92 million incurred towards operating our manufacturing facilities.
*Restated profit before tax from continuing operations*  . As a result of the foregoing, our restated profit before tax from continuing operations was ₹2,218.47 million for the nine months period ended September 30, 2025.
