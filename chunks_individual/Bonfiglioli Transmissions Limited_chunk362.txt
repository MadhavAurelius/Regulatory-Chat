*Direct Customer Relationships*
Our direct customers are predominantly OEMs engaged in the end-user verticals of material handling, logistics and stocking systems, cranes and winches, material working, recycling and sustainability, aggregates building, packaging and labelling, mining, biogas, textiles, conveying and robotics . Our top 10 customers accounted for less than 47% of revenue from operations in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and in each of Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022.
In the nine months period ended September 30, 2025, we serviced 1,181 customers of which 1,114 customers were based in India.

*Dealer Network*
We engage with dealers to access a wide base of OEMs for our Industry & Automation vertical. As of September 30, 2025, our network comprised 44 authorized dealers, of which 36 were engaged by us on an exclusive basis. Furthermore, 16 of our dealers had been associated with us for 20 or more years as of September 30, 2025. Our network of 44 dealers operated 54 outlets across India as of September 30, 2025. In addition, as of September 30, 2025, nine dealers were additionally authorized to address after-sales service requirements of our customers.
