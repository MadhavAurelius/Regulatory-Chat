# Appeal No. 6704 of 2026

## **ORDER**

1.  The appellant had filed an application dated January 01, 2026 (received by the respondent through RTI MIS Portal) under the Right to Information Act, 2005 (“  **RTI Act**  ”). The respondent, by a letter dated January 19, 2026, responded to the application filed by the appellant. The appellant filed an appeal (Reg. No. SEBIH/A/E/26/00026) dated January 21, 2026. I have carefully considered the application, the response and the appeal and find that the matter can be decided based on the material available on record.
2.   **Queries in the application**  - The appellant, in his application dated January 01, 2026, sought the following information regarding allotment process in the Initial Public Offering (IPO) of E2E Networks Limited, listed on NSE Emerge SME Platform:
*“ A. GENERAL IPO DETAILS:*
*Complete details of the IPO issue including:*
*Issue opening and closing dates*
*Issue size and price band*
*Total number of shares offered under QIB category*
*Total applications received under QIB category*
*Total number of shares applied for under QIB category*
*Final subscription status category-wise under QIB category*
*B. QIB CATEGORY ALLOTMENT DETAILS:*
*Complete basis of allotment document/report prepared by the Registrar (MUFG InTime India Private Limited) specifically* *for the QIB category, including:*
*Methodology adopted for allotment in QIB category*
*Whether proportionate allotment basis was followed as per SEBI (ICDR) Regulations*
*Detailed working of the allotment formula used*
*Category-wise bifurcation of QIB applicants (Mutual Funds, Insurance Companies, FPIs, etc.)*
*Total number of QIB applications received and their details:*
*Number of applications received*
*Number of valid applications*
*Number of applications rejected (with reasons)*
*Minimum and maximum application size in QIB category*
*Final allotment statistics for QIB category:*
*Total number of QIBs allotted shares*
*Total number of shares allotted in QIB category*
*Percentage of allotment in QIB category*
*Details of any discretionary allotment, if applicable*
*C. PROCESS AND COMPLIANCE VERIFICATION:*
*Copy of the final basis of allotment approval granted by the NSE Emerge/Stock Exchange for the QIB category.*
*Confirmation whether the allotment in QIB category was done on proportionate basis as mandated under Regulation 253 of* *SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018, or any other applicable regulation for SME platform.*
