## * **Fiscal 2023 compared to Fiscal 2022** *

Materials Cost (continuing operations). Materials Cost (continuing operations) increased from ₹9,921.01 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹10,843.66 million in Fiscal 2023 and represented 63.65% of our revenue from operations (continuing operations) in Fiscal 2022 and 64.26% of our revenue from operations (continuing operations) in Fiscal 2023. This was primarily attributable to increase in purchase price of raw material/components cost.
Employee benefits expense (continuing operations). Employee benefits expense (continuing operations) increased by 11.91% from ₹800.56 million for Fiscal 2022 to ₹895.93 million for Fiscal 2023, primarily due to increases in

headcount from 698 employees as of December 31, 2022 to 752 employees as of December 31, 2023, and increases in salaries, wages and bonus by 15.87% from ₹677.21 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹784.66 million in Fiscal 2023 on account of annual increments.
The increase in employee benefits expense was partially offset by a decline in gratuity expense, on account of increased attrition and reduction in discount rate.
Depreciation and amortisation expense (continuing operatio n s ) . Depreciation and amortisation expense (continuing operations) was ₹337.75 million for Fiscal 2022 and ₹363.07 million for Fiscal 2023.
Finance costs (continuing operations) . Finance costs (continuing operations) increased by 84.40% from ₹70.76 million for Fiscal 2022 to ₹130.48 million for Fiscal 2023, driven mainly by an increase in interest on borrowings, which increased by 135.95% from ₹32.88 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹77.58 million in Fiscal 2023, as a result of higher borrowing from banks to avail export packing credit for working capital purposes and higher other borrowings (bills discounting). Other borrowing costs increased by 49.12% from ₹31.90 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹47.57 million in Fiscal 2023.

-  Professional fees, which increased by 18.37% from ₹401.37 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹475.11 million in

 Fiscal 2023, as a result of higher cost sharing expenses to BSPA, higher industrial and corporate consultancy.
-  Royalty, which increased by 15.09% from ₹653.69 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹752.35 million in Fiscal 2023,

 due to increase in sales
-  Contract labour charges, which increased by 45.92% from ₹50.02 million in Fiscal 2022 to ₹72.99 million in

 Fiscal 2023, on account of higher deployment of temporary workmen to meet the higher production
-  Repairs and maintenance on plant and machinery, which increased by 41.08% from ₹78.48 million in Fiscal

 2022 to ₹110.72 million in Fiscal 2023.
*Tax expense (continuing operations).*  Total tax expense (continuing operations) increased by 9.17% from ₹563.67 million for Fiscal 2022 to ₹615.36 million for Fiscal 2023. This was expense is primarily attributable to tax pertaining to earlier years changing from a credit of ₹12.47 million in Fiscal 2022 to a tax of ₹73.52 million in Fiscal 2023.
