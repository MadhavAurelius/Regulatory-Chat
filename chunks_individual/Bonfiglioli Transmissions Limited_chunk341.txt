## * **Highly diversified offerings across business verticals and customer industries** *

We manufacture a wide range of mission-critical and highly precision engineered gear motors, gearboxes and drive systems used across diverse end-user markets (  *Source: F&S Report*  ), across Industry & Automation, Off- Highway Mobility and Wind Industries. As of September 30, 2025, we manufactured and supplied more than 10,500 SKUs and were the only major gearbox supplier with a comprehensive portfolio serving Industry & Automation, Off-Highway Mobility and Wind end-markets (  *Source: F&S Report*  ).

Low-medium duty gear motors find applications in equipment such as agitators, blenders, dryers, conveying systems and extruders in a wide range of end-user industries including material handling, chemicals and pharmaceuticals, mining, food, beverage and tobacco, metal processing, among others.  As a result of our wide range of products we are able to cater to customers across industries, and no single end-user industry contributed more than 10% of our revenue from the Industry & Automation vertical in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and in Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022.
