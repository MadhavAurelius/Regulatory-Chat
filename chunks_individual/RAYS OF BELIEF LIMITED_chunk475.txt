## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### **Our Centres**

*3.*    *School Collaboration Centres*
These centres are set up within existing preschool or school campuses in collaboration with the schools to provide services to the children enrolled in those schools. The operational structure and revenue model for these type of centres are as follows:
i.  Operational Structure:
Our Company establishes therapy and early intervention services inside partner schools, creating convenient access to care for enrolled students. The school provides physical infrastructure while our Company provides clinical operations and staffing. The Company and partner schools collaborate to organize awareness programs and educational workshops and training sessions for staff and teachers to help them make the school environment more welcoming for neurodivergent children.
ii.  Revenue Model:
Our Company pays a pre-negotiated rental fee and/or a revenue share to the school as per the terms and conditions of their agreement. Majority of the service revenue is retained by our Company, and the remaining is remitted to the school as per the agreement. This model allows access to a concentrated child demographic and supports school-readiness interventions.
In addition to these centres, we have established an  *“Upskilling Academy”*  and a  *“Centre for Excellence and* *Research” (“*   * **COER** *   *”)*  :
The  *“Upskilling Academy”*  has been modelled on the principles of Continuing Education (“  **CE**  ”). The academy provides structured and ongoing training programs for our clinical professionals across our Company. These continuous upskilling programs ensure that every team member is equipped with the latest methodologies, clinical protocols, and behavioural frameworks required to deliver high-impact therapy.
The  *“COER”*  is a flagship centre designed to drive innovation in therapy and care delivery for neurodivergent children. This centre functions as our clinical and research hub where advanced therapeutic models, technologies, and service processes are developed and piloted in collaboration with experts and partners.
