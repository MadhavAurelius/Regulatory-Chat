## **11. Psychotherapy and Counselling**

-   **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)**  : Often used for individuals with ADHD, anxiety, and

 depression, CBT helps in modifying negative thought patterns and developing coping strategies for managing symptoms.

-   **Play Therapy**  : Used particularly for younger children with ASD or emotional difficulties, play

 therapy allows children to express themselves and address social, emotional, or behavioural concerns in a safe environment.
