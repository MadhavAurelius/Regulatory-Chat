## **Risk factors**

Investors are advised to read the risk factors carefully before taking an investment decision in the Offer. Certain significant risk factors include the following:
-  We depend on a limited number of suppliers for procurement of raw materials and components required for

 our manufacturing operations, and purchases from our top 10 suppliers represented 41.92%, 44.64%, 47.21% & 48.06% of our total purchases of raw materials and components in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and in Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022, respectively. As we do not have definitive agreements with our suppliers, any interruption in the availability of raw materials and components on account of any disruption, breakdown or shutdown of our suppliers’ operations could adversely impact our operations.
-  Any increase in the prices of raw materials and components required for our operations or levy of duties on

 the materials we import for our operations, could adversely affect our business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows.
-  Any unscheduled, unplanned or prolonged disruption of operations at our manufacturing facilities could

 materially and adversely affect our business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows.
-  Any actual or perceived defects in our gearboxes and drive systems or the sales and after-sale services

 provided through dealers or third parties may adversely impact our brand, reputation, and ability to sell our products, which could have an adverse impact on our operations.

-  We depend on Bonfiglioli S.p.A., one of our Promoters, for certain aspects of our operations. Any adverse

 change in our relationship with Bonfiglioli S.p.A. and the companies in the Bonfiglioli Group could have an adverse impact on our business, financial condition, results of operations, cash flows, and reputation.
-  We have entered into and will continue to enter into related party transactions with Bonfiglioli S.p.A. and

 companies within the Bonfiglioli Group that may involve conflicts of interest, which could adversely impact our business.
-  We depend on a limited number of customers for our sales, and revenue from our top 10 customers

 represented 46.35%, 43.16%, 41.08% and 39.81% of our total revenue from operations (continuing operations) in the nine month period ended September 30, 2025 and in Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022, respectively. As we do not have long-term binding agreements with all our customers, any reduction in orders from, or loss of, any of our major customers could adversely affect our business, financial condition, results of operations and cash flows.
-  Failure to successfully commercialise co-engineered products could adversely affect our business, financial

 condition, results of operations, and cash flows.
-  We may not be able to compete successfully in the highly competitive, price sensitive, and fast evolving

 market for gearboxes and drive systems which could have an adverse impact on our operations.
-  Our inability to accurately forecast order volumes or optimally schedule production, may lead to under or

 over capacity utilisation, which could adversely affect our manufacturing schedules and related costs.
