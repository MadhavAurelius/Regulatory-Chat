## **11. Psychotherapy and Counselling**

#### **Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) Overview**
Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a complex developmental condition that affects an individual's cognitive processes, behaviour, and social interactions. The term "spectrum" reflects the wide- ranging manifestations of the disorder, with symptoms varying significantly across individuals in terms of severity, abilities, and challenges. While the core features of ASD include difficulties with communication, social engagement, and behaviour, the intensity of these symptoms can differ from one person to another.
