## * **The Steel and Steel Products (Quality Control) Order, 2024** *
The Steel and Steel Products (Quality Control) Order, 2024 (the “  **QC Order**  ”) was issued by the Ministry of Steel, Government of India, to mandate certification by the BIS for specified steel products. All manufacturers of steel and steel products must obtain certification and ensure adherence to the QC Order. The QC Order further stipulates that certain steel and steel products identified therein shall bear the Standard Mark under a license from the BIS, in accordance with the Bureau of Indian Standards (Conformity Assessment) Regulations, 2018. Any sub-standard or defective steel or steel product that does not conform to the prescribed standard must be disposed of as scrap in a manner that ensures compliance with the Bureau of Indian Standards Act, 2016. Any person who violates any provision of the QC Order is liable to punishment under section 29 of the BIS Act.
