## * **Global Industrial Gearboxes -  New and Replacement shipyard Demand** *
The global industrial gearboxes market is broadly characterized by two primary demand segments - new installation demand and replacement or refurbishment demand. New demand accounts for around 45-50% of total market volume. Growth in new manufacturing facilities, renewable energy installations, and infrastructure development projects is generating substantial opportunities. Moreover, increasing investments in process industries (Cement, Steel) and wind energy are expanding their use of compact planetary and helical gearboxes. New demand is particularly strong in Asia-Pacific and China, with India seeing consistent traction driven by domestic manufacturing growth and government-led initiatives.
In contrast, replacement demand represents the larger share of the market approximately 50-55% driven by the aging machinery base in developed economies (Europe, North America) and the shift toward energy-efficient,

low-maintenance solutions. Overall, while replacement demand currently dominates the market, new installation demand is expanding faster.
