## * **Agreements with BSPA; License Agreement and Intercompany Services** *

BSPA owns the Bonfiglioli technology, know-how, production and trade secrets of a majority of the products we manufacture, and the “Bonfiglioli” trade name and trademark. We therefore depend on the intellectual property that is economically and legally owned by BSPA for the license to manufacture and supply a majority of our products, which has been granted to us and is governed by a License for Limited Scope Manufacturing Agreement dated June 1, 2021 entered into with BSPA (together with amendments thereto, the “  **License Agreement**  ”). The License Agreement has a validity of one year and is automatically renewed for successive one-year terms.
Under the License Agreement, we have been granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable and non-sublicensable license to use BSPA’s intellectual property relating to specified products, as well the right to use the Bonfiglioli trade name and trademark for manufacturing and sale of such products in the specified territories of India, Nepal, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Sri Lanka and subject to BSPA’s prior consent, any other country. The license has been granted to us subject to certain terms and conditions for a consideration of 6% of our annual sales of the specified products (excluding sales to BSPA and net of discounts and incentives). The License Agreement has a validity of one year and is automatically renewed for successive one-year terms.
