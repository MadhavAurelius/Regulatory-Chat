## **Strengths**

* **End-to-end technology-led power transmissions and industrial drive solutions provider across business** * * **verticals** *
We are the largest manufacturer of gearboxes and drives for OEMs and end-users engaged in Industry & Automation (gear motors < 15 kNm), Off-Highway Mobility and Wind sectors in India as of September 30, 2025, in terms of revenue from operations, according to the F&S Report. We attribute our market leadership position to our capability as an end-to-end technology-led power transmissions and co-engineered industrial drive solutions provider. Our current capabilities span designing, manufacturing and supplying products from motion control to power drives including gearboxes, gear motors, inverter controls, servo motors, servo cables and accessories.
For instance, we are able to provide complete power and control solutions for battery swapping applications in electric vehicles at charging stations, which included gear motors, inverters, cables and accessories to facilitate five different types of movements/ functions in the swapping operation (vertical, horizontal, indexing, forking and push/ pull):

The breadth of our product portfolio enables us to consistently meet our customers’ specific requirements, while leveraging on economies of scale in terms of our vendor relationships. We have strengthened our position in the Off-Highway Mobility Industry and Wind Industry verticals through our co-engineering and customization services that help us gain an in-depth understanding of the market specific challenges of our customers, which in turn helps us further refine our offerings for the Indian market. Some of our customization initiatives including developing drives for large wind turbines of 5MW capacity, indigenous tracked paddy harvesters for agricultural application, large girder transporters and developing track and slew drives for mounted cranes, indigenous pavers and hydraulic excavators.
