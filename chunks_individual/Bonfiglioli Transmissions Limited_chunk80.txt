We enter into agreements with our dealers for one-year terms that are automatically renewed yearly, and as a result, have long-standing relationships with most of our dealers. As of September 30, 2025, 16 out of 44 dealers had been engaged with us for 20 years or more. We are therefore dependent on our existing dealer network to the extent of the revenue we generate from sales to the network. Our dealers are incentivized through discounts they are entitled to claim depending on the sales they make. Our dealers’ continued association with us is dependent to a large extent on our brand, price competitiveness, efficient and timely product deliveries, and consistent product quality, all of which impact the marketability of our products and therefore volume of sales by dealers. If we are unable to provide adequate gearboxes to meet the demand of our dealers for sales or after-sales services due to insufficient manufacturing capacity, miscommunications, inaccurate demand forecast or otherwise, our sales and after-sale services could be negatively affected. In addition, some of our dealers are not engaged with us on an exclusive basis and have the ability to distribute products of our competitors in India. If our relationship with a dealer is terminated or if a dealer prefers to focus on its business with our competitors and reduces purchases of our gearboxes, we may not be able to replace such relationship with a new dealer in a timely manner or at all. In addition, any such new relationship may take time to develop and may not be as favourable to us as the relationship it is replacing.
Furthermore, since dealers have direct contact with our customers when they purchase our gearboxes and during the after-sale services, their conduct has a significant impact on the customers’ perception of our gearboxes and our brand. Our dealer network may also be affected by issues affecting dealers such as bankruptcy, non- compliance of legal requirements, failure of their information systems or any security breach or any labour unrest and slowdowns, and the adverse effects of the seasonality of sales, could lead to fluctuations in our revenue and could also negatively affect both, our financial performance and our reputation in the industry and amongst customers. For instance, we have previously had some dealers default on their payment obligations towards us for an amount of ₹5.69 million in Fiscal 2017. We have since initiated legal proceedings against such dealers to recover the sum owed to us. There can be no assurance that the legal proceedings will be decided in our favour or that we will not experience similar issues with our existing dealer network in the future. Any such disputes in the future or any failure to renew our existing arrangements with our dealer network on similar terms or at all, could impact our ability to access end-customers across a wide range of industries, which could adversely affect our business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows.
