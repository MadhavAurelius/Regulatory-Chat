## **Currency and Units of Presentation**
All references to “Rupee(s)”, “Rs.” or “₹” or “INR” are to Indian Rupees, the official currency of the Republic of India. All references to “US$” or “U.S. Dollars” or “USD” are to United States Dollars, the official currency of the United States of America. All references to “EUR” in this Draft Red Herring Prospectus are to the Euro, the official currency of the Italian Republic, a member of the European Union.
Our Company has presented certain numerical information in this Draft Red Herring Prospectus in ‘million’ units or in whole numbers where the numbers have been too small to represent in such units. One million represents 1,000,000, one billion represents 1,000,000,000 and one trillion represents 1,000,000,000,000.
