## **A. Shift Toward Advanced Gearbox Types & High-Efficiency Gear Motors**
Planetary units are gaining traction for their torque density and compactness, while helical and helical- bevel gearboxes remain the backbone of heavy-duty applications. Worm gearboxes are losing relevance in high-efficiency environments. Across segments, OEMs are moving toward IE4/IE5 gear motors with integrated smart drives to reduce energy use and lifecycle costs.
