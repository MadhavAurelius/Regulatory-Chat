# Adjudication Order in respect of Amit Agarwal in the matter of dealings in Illiquid Stock Options on[...]

3. During the IP, 14,720 entities were found to have executed non-genuine trades in BSE’s stock options segment. It was observed that Amit Agarwal (hereinafter referred to as the  **“Noticee”**  ) was one of the entities who indulged in execution of reversal trades in stock options segment of BSE during the IP. The Noticee’s trades were alleged to be non-genuine in nature which created false or misleading appearance of trading in terms of artificial volumes in stock options. Therefore, Noticee’s trades were alleged to be manipulative and deceptive in nature. In view of the same, SEBI initiated adjudication proceedings against the Noticee for alleged violation of the provisions of regulations 3(a), (b), (c), (d), 4(1) and 4(2)(a) of SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices) Regulations, 2003 (hereinafter referred to as “  **PFUTP Regulations**  ”).
