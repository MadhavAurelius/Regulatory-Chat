## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Programs we offer** *

Our Company provides subscription-based, guided therapy programs designed to empower parents of children with special needs. We support children from 18 months up to 12 years of age, recognizing that developmental needs vary significantly at each stage.
Our programs are age specific and condition specific, offering structured guidance and therapy across the following segments:
i.   *Early Intervention (Infant and Toddler) Programs (18 months to 2 years):*  This program focuses on infants and toddlers, addressing early signs of developmental delays. Parents are offered training and individual support on how to stimulate a toddler's cognitive and language abilities, emotions.
ii.   *Preschool Programs (3 to 5 years)*  : This program is tailored for school readiness. This program builds foundational academic, behavioural, and communication skills through play-based learning and structured therapy.
iii.   *School-Age Programs (6 to 12 years)*  : This program supports children navigating academic and social challenges. We offer targeted therapies such as special education, language and communication therapy, occupational therapy, and ABA.
iv.   *Adolescent Programs (13 to 15 years)*  : This program is designed to support teens with self-regulation, executive functioning, social-emotional skills, and transition planning for academic and vocational settings.
Our therapy portfolio includes occupational therapy, speech and language therapy, ABA based behaviour modification support and special education, ensuring a comprehensive, individualized care path. These therapies help the children in the following way:
*i.*    *Occupational Therapy:*  It serves as a link between play and developmental milestones, nurturing essential life skills and helping in empowering them to achieve independence.
*ii.*    *Speech and Language Therapy:*  A specialised intervention to enhance communication skills, addressing speech and language disorders to facilitate effective expression and interaction.
*iii.*    *Behavioural Therapy:*  A targeted intervention strategy that addresses and modifies behavioural patterns, promoting positive changes and enhancing a child’s ability to navigate social environments.
*iv.*    *Special Education:*  A personalised and inclusive educational framework designed to meet the needs of children with learning challenges, fostering their academic and social development.
