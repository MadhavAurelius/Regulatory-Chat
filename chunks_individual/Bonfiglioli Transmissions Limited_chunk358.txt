## * **Wind Industry** *

We were among the earliest companies to establish domestic manufacturing of wind-turbine pitch and yaw drives in India, beginning in 2006 at our facility in Chennai (Tamil Nadu) (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). We specialize in manufacturing yaw and pitch drives for wind turbines and have a market leading share of approximately 70% among domestic manufacturers in terms of sales volumes  in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025, in India (  *Source: F&S Report*  ).

*Products*
We manufacture yaw and pitch drives for wind turbines with capacities ranging from 225kw to 5mw, and have established and proven capabilities to design, manufacture, and supply fully integrated yaw and pitch drive systems for wind turbines at scale (  *Source: F&S Report*  ).

*Innovation*
We introduced our first-generation pitch drive in 2006, which was followed by the second generation in 2009 with added features such as high torque density, ease for assembly with less components, compact drive solution, cost effective solution and new materials.
We currently manufacture our third-generation yaw and pitch drive with a patented ring nut design.
*Products Co-Engineered with Customers*
We develop bespoke products for our customers through a process involving requirement definition, product design and development, prototyping, product and process validation, prototype/start of production (“  **SOP**  ”) certification, and industrialization. The intellectual property for the co-engineered product remains with the Bonfiglioli Group.
