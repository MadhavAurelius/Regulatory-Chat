## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### **OVERVIEW ON INDIAN DEVELOPMENTAL DELAY (DD) MARKET**
Developmental delay refers to when a child is not meeting typical developmental milestones within the expected time frames. These milestones include skills and abilities related to physical, cognitive, communication, social, and emotional development. A developmental delay can be identified in one or more of these areas.
