## * **Global Industrial Gearboxes Market: Segmentation by Product Type** *
The global industrial gearbox market is dominated by helical gearboxes (41.0%), followed by bevel (26.9%), planetary (31.1%), and worm (1.0%) types. Helical and bevel gearboxes lead due to their efficiency and versatility, while planetary units are the fastest growing owing to their efficiency and high torque density.
