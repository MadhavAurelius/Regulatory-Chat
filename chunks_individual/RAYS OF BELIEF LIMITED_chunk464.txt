## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Continued expansion of our network with a focus on driving cost efficiencies across our operations** *

We intend to establish behaviour therapy support services in our three acquired centres in the USA over the next three years. We aim to further expand our operations in the countries of United Arab Emirates and United Kingdom  as well. Group Companies, Carving Futures SPV Limited and Carving Futures UK Private Limited, have been incorporated in the United Arab Emirates and the United Kingdom, respectively, to help us expand our operations in these countries.
* **Attracting and retaining qualified clinical professionals through continuous training, knowledge sharing and** * * **upskilling** *
The success of our operations is highly dependent the competence and skill of our clinical professionals. As we expand further, we will have to enhance our measures for recruitment, training, upskilling, and retention of clinical professionals.
As a part of such endeavour, we aim to continue providing training and upskilling opportunities to our clinical professionals through CME and other workshops and webinars. We will also continue providing a platform to our clinical professionals by organising workshops and webinars. We strive to only enhance our commitment to imparting the best services by providing to our clinical professionals, comprehensive training and upskilling opportunities, and further attracting high-quality talent.
As part of the Objects of the Issue, we propose to set up seven new  *“Upskilling Academies”*  . The  *“Upskilling* *Academies”*  would play a pivotal role in maintaining service excellence, enhancing clinical outcomes, and fostering a culture of lifelong learning and professional growth within our Company. For further details, please see “  *Our* *Business - Our Centres*  ” and “  *Objects of the Issue*  ” on pages 235 and 115. We plan to continue taking more such steps to attract and retain skilled clinical professionals.
