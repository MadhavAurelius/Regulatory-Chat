## **Senior Management**

**Pranesh Kumar**  is the Vice President of Sourcing and Purchase of our Company and he is responsible for sourcing and purchasing functions in our Company. He has been associated with our Company since April 2025. He holds a bachelor’s of engineering degree in mechanical engineering from Visveswaraiah Technological University, Belgaum, Karnataka, a diploma in mechanical engineering from the Board of Technical Examinations, Karnataka, a diploma in materials management from Indian Institute of Management and Technology, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, and has completed an executive general management programme from the Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore. He was previously associated with BS Appliances Limited, IFB Automotive Private Limited, Siemens VDO Automotive, Honeywell Turbo (India) Private Limited, Volvo India Private Limited, and Ingersoll Rand India Private Limited. He has over 28 years of experience in sourcing and purchasing functions. In Fiscal 2025, he received an aggregate compensation of ₹ 6.91 million from our Company  and no compensation has been accrued for Fiscal 2025 which is payable at a later date.
**Loganathan Nagaraj**  is the Director of Manufacturing in our Company and he is responsible for manufacturing operations in our Company. He has been associated with our Company since February 2019. He holds a degree in bachelor’s of engineering in mechanical engineering from the University of Madras and a degree in master of science in quality management from the Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, Rajasthan. He was previously associated with Rane Engine Valve Limited, WABCO India Limited, and Lear Automotive India

Private Limited. He has over 25 years of experience in operations and manufacturing functions. In Fiscal 2025, he received an aggregate compensation of ₹ 9.97 million from our Company  and no compensation has been accrued for Fiscal 2025 which is payable at a later date.
**Priti Harihar Dave**  is the Deputy General Manager - Marketing and Communications in our Company and she is responsible for overseeing marketing and corporate communications of our Company. She has been associated with our Company since August 2016. She holds a bachelor of arts degree from Osmania University, Telangana, a post graduate diploma in business administration with specialisations in marketing management and human resource development from Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning, Pune, Maharashtra, and is certified in digital marketing from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi. She was previously associated with the India branch office of Epson Singapore Pte Limited, Ditium Technologies Limited, FFE Minerals India Private Limited, GETIT Infomediary Limited, Makino India Private Limited, Danfoss Industries Private Limited, Rittal India Private Limited, and National Engineering Industries Limited. She has over 27 years of experience in marketing and communications. In Fiscal 2025, she received an aggregate compensation of ₹ 6.10 million from our Company and no compensation has been accrued for Fiscal 2025 which is payable at a later date.
