## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### **Table 17: Mental Health and Disability Policy**
|  Program/Initiative  |  Description  |
|:---|:---|
| National Mental Health Program  (NMHP)  | Focuses on improving access to mental health services, including for children  with neurodevelopmental disorders (NDDs). However, attention to NDDs is  often limited to their inclusion within broader mental health conditions. |
| National Action Plan for Inclusion (2017)  | Aims to enhance social inclusion for persons with disabilities. Includes  provisions for children with NDDs, particularly in education and healthcare  access.  |
| National Trust for the Welfare of  Persons with Autism, Cerebral Palsy,  Intellectual Disabilities, and Multiple  Disabilities | A statutory body that advocates for the rights of individuals with certain  NDDs. Provides grants for specialized institutions and NGOs, focusing on  advocacy, legal rights, and support for individuals with disabilities.  |
