Our cost of raw materials and components consumed are generally impacted by our manufacturing volumes, mix of products, the prices paid for raw materials and our manufacturing efficiency. Our primary raw materials required for the manufacture of our products include iron and aluminium castings, forgings, gears, bearings and proprietary components. Our suppliers may impose price increases and surcharges on us from time to time. In addition, according to the F&S Report, pricing and availability of such materials can be highly volatile due to numerous factors beyond the control of any individual company, including general economic conditions, global production capacity, import levels, fluctuations in raw material costs, sales levels, competition, supply chain fluctuations, labour costs, trade policies, and foreign currency exchange rates.
