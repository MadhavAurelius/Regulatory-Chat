## **Overview**

As of September 30, 2025, we operated three facilities in India, two of which were in Tamil Nadu (at Thirumudivakkam and Mannur) and one was in Maharashtra (in Talegaon), that manufactured a combined total of 330,057 units of gear motors and gearboxes in Fiscal 2024. Over the years, we have consistently increased sourcing parts and materials from domestic suppliers to strengthen our localisation strategy. As of September 30, 2025, we had 186 suppliers in India and 69 suppliers outside India. In the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and in Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022, purchases of raw materials and components from the domestic market represented 70.70%, 68.81%, 64.97% and 67.04% of our total purchase of raw material and components, respectively, demonstrating our commitment to sourcing from India. We source from a diverse network of suppliers, with no single supplier providing more than 20.00% of our purchases in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022. For further information on our material procurement, see “  * **-  Our Operations -  Procurement and Suppliers** *  ” on page 216.
We follow a multi-dimensional approach to quality assurance that involves ensuring quality compliance at each level of our operations. We also provide after-sales services to our customers through two dedicated channels: (a) team of in-house service engineers stationed at our 13 sales offices across India (three of which are located at our manufacturing locations in Thirumudivakkam (Tamil Nadu), Mannur (Tamil Nadu), and Talegaon (Maharashtra)

that aggregated to 15 engineers as of September 30, 2025; and (b) a broad base of 13 third-party authorised service facilities that had an aggregate of 26 service engineers as of September 30, 2025. We strive to, on average, respond to complaints within the next working day, significantly increasing product uptime and lifecycle value.
Our 25 years of operating experience in India, global R&D, manufacturing capabilities, and customer relationships, have enabled us to deliver growth over the years in terms of our profitability, return on equity and return on capital employed.
