|  1 | Capital  Requirements | i.  Our Company proposes to venture into production of the edible Soybean Oil by  forward integration i.e. setting up of Refining Plant and Bottling Plant. The vendor for the proposed expansion has been finalized, quotations have been  received and approved by the management, and the necessary purchase orders  have been placed. The installation and implementation of the 5 MW Solar Power  Plant is currently under progress. Accordingly, capital is required for the civil  construction as well purchase and installation of the requisite solar units for the  generation of the solar electricity. However, the funds for the same would be  organized from Internal accruals and/or from Banks/Financial institutions. |
