## **Utilisation of the Offer Proceeds by the Promoter Selling Shareholder**
Our Company will not receive any proceeds from the Offer (the “  **Offer Proceeds**  ”) and all the Offer Proceeds will be received by the Promoter Selling Shareholder after deduction of Offer related expenses and relevant taxes thereon, to be borne by the Promoter Selling Shareholder.
