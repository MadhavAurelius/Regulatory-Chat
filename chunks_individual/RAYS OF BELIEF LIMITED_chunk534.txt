#### * **Rehabilitation Laws** *
The Rehabilitation Council of India Act, 1992 establishes the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI) to regulate and monitor the training and professional standards of rehabilitation professionals and personnel, including clinical psychologists, special educators, and other allied fields. It mandates the recognition of qualifications, maintenance of a Central Rehabilitation Register, and adherence to minimum standards of education and professional conduct. The Act also ensures accountability through licensing, periodic inspections, and withdrawal of recognition for non- compliance. By promoting standardized practices, it safeguards the rights and welfare of persons with disabilities, ensuring access to competent and ethical rehabilitation services across India.
