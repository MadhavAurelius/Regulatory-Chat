## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Marketing and Outreach Strategy** *
Our Company employs a structured, multi-channel outreach and engagement strategy built on the principle of early awareness, trust-building, and inclusive access. Rather than traditional commercial marketing, our approach is rooted in advocacy, education, and enabling families to take timely, informed decisions about their child's developmental needs.
Our marketing and outreach strategy operates across three integrated pathways—ATL, BTL, and Medical-Backed (“  **MB**  ”) initiatives. These are structured to complement the Parent Experience Journey, a framework that guides families from the earliest stages of concern to meaningful developmental progress.
Our strategy is built around creating awareness, enabling early identification, and guiding families into supportive care. This approach aligns with our ethical framework and therapeutic philosophy.
