## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **The Parent Experience Journey** *

Our Company’s outreach strategy centres around the “Parent Experience Journey”, comprising five sequential phases that govern and inform every mode of stakeholder engagement whether conducted via digital platforms, in-person interactions, or clinical services, without limitation. The phases are as follows:
1.   *Awareness and Recognition*  :
The initial phase involves the dissemination of information regarding developmental milestones and potential indicators of developmental challenges. This awareness is facilitated through digital content, strategic partnerships with preschools, and community outreach initiatives. The objective of this phase is to reduce stigma associated with developmental issues and to encourage an informed and proactive approach among parents and guardians.
2.   *Screening and Assessment*  :
Upon achieving adequate awareness, parents and guardians are granted access to developmental screening tools. These tools include standardized assessment tools. The assessments are available both online and at designated centres to ensure accessibility and convenience.
3.   *Consultation and Plan Design*  :
Following the completion of the assessment, the clinical team formulates an IEP or IGP for the child. The plans are constructed based on clinical evaluations and parental input. During this phase, parents are provided with detailed consultations, tailored tools, and comprehensive orientation regarding the plan’s implementation and anticipated outcomes.
4.   *Therapy and Parent Empowerment*  :
The therapeutic phase commences with targeted interventions tailored to the child's specific needs. This stage emphasizes the active involvement of parents through structured coaching sessions, the provision of customized play kits, and systematic progress tracking through monthly updates. Parent training sessions, emotional support resources, and facilitated community engagement groups are integral to fostering caregiver empowerment and sustained involvement.
* **5.** *    *Review and Reintegration*  :
Our clinical team regularly reviews measurable progress to evaluate goal attainment and developmental advancements. They issue progress reports accordingly and provide support to facilitate the child’s reintegration into educational settings or social inclusion initiatives.
