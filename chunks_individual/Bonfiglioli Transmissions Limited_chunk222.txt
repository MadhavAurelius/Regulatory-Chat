## **Special indirect tax benefits available to the Company**

The Company is availing/ eligible for the following benefits under Indirect Tax Laws:
-  With respect to export of goods made from its units in Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra, the Company is availing

 the benefit of remission of duties, taxes and other levies at the Central, State and local level which are borne on the exported goods manufactured in India under Remission of Duties and Taxes on Exported Products (‘RoDTEP’) scheme issued through Notification no. 19/2015-2020 dated 17 August 2021 by Ministry of Commerce & Industry under Department of Commerce.
-  The Company is availing the benefit of duty drawback with respect to duty paid on import of materials used

 in manufacture of export goods as per Duty Drawback scheme under 75 of Custom Act, 1962.
-  The Company is availing the benefit of duty-free import of capital goods as per Export Promotion Capital

 Goods Scheme under the Foreign Trade Policy, 2023, subject to fulfillment of export obligations within time as provided under the scheme.
-  The Company has obtained registration under the GST law in eight States. The Company undertakes export

 of goods from its unit registered in Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra. Under the GST regime, supplies of goods which are exported outside India are treated as zero-rated supplies. Such zero-rated supplies of goods are allowed to be made under either of the following options:
o  Without payment of Integrated Goods and Services Tax ("IGST") under Bond/ Letter of Undertaking (LUT). Under this scenario, the exporter is allowed to claim refund of unutilized input tax credit.
