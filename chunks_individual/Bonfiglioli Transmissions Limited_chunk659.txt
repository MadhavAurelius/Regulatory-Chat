## **Dividend Payment**
Final dividend distributed to equity shareholders is recognised in the period in which it is approved by our members in the Annual General Meeting. Interim Dividend is recognised when approved by our Board of Directors at the board meeting. Dividend distributed (including interim dividend) is recognised in the statement of changes in equity.
