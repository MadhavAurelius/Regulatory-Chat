## **India Industrial Gear Motors Market: Segmentation by Product Type**

India’s industrial gear motors market is dominated by helical gear motors, reflecting their superior efficiency, compact design, and versatility across manufacturing and process industries. Bevel gear motors constitute the next significant segment, gaining traction in cranes, lifting equipment, and heavy-duty material-handling applications, supported by rising infrastructure and logistics investments.
