# The Company has provided loan to Bonfiglioli Technology Space Private Limited (i) amounting to Rs 115.50 (December 31, 2024 - Rs 115.50; December 31, 2023 - Nil; December 31, 2022 - Nil) relating to sale of discontinued operations, which is repayable in 5 equal annual instalments at an interest rate of 8.25% p.a and (ii) cash pooling facility amounting to Rs 12.96 (December 31, 2024- Rs. 103.01; December 31, 2023 - Nil; December 31, 2022 - Nil) at an interest rate of 8.25%, repayable within a year.

## **Expenses**

*Finance costs.*  Finance costs include interest on borrowings, interest on lease liabilities, and other borrowing costs.
*Depreciation and amortisation expense.*  Depreciation and amortisation expense relate to depreciation of property, plant and equipment, and amortisation of intangible assets and right-of-use-assets.
*Other expenses.*  Other expenses primarily comprise (i) royalty to the Bonfiglioli Group for use of intellectual property including the “Bonfiglioli” brand, (ii) professional fees incurred towards cost sharing from Bonfiglioli Group under the Intra-Group Services Agreement and other third party consultancy for industrial and corporate functions, (iii) sub-contracting charges incurred towards processing of raw materials that we outsource to third party manufacturers,(iv) power and fuel expenses, and (v) consumption of stores, spare parts and tools. For further information, see “  * **Our Business -  Our Operations -  Agreements with BSPA; License Agreement and** * * **Intercompany Services** *   *”*  on page 212.
Other expenses also comprise expenses towards rent, repairs and maintenance on plant and machinery, buildings and others, insurance, rates and taxes, travelling and conveyance expenses, contract labour charges, payments to auditors, directors’ remuneration, expenditure towards corporate social responsibility (“  **CSR**  ”) activities, freight and handling charges (net), provision for product warranty, allowance for doubtful debts, communication expenses, bad debts written off, asset written off, provision for sales tax disputes, net loss on foreign currency transactions and translation, and miscellaneous expenses.
