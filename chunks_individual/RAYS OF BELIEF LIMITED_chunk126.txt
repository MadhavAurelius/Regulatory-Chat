As a provider of services that require clients to come to the centres for availing of therapy programs, we are significantly exposed to the public health and economic effects of events such as the COVID-19 pandemic. The impact of pandemics or public health emergencies on our business, operations and financial performance may include, but is not limited to:
a.  adverse impact on the cash flows and access to funds for our potential and existing clients, which may affect their demand for our offerings and adversely affect our Revenue from Operations; b.  restrictions on movement of people and necessary materials required for our business operations as well as risk of our employees contracting the disease; c.  temporary or permanent refusal of employees to return to work and their failure to return our equipment; technology and equipment shortages due to global supply chain issues; and d.  increased costs to ensure the safety of our workforce and continuity of operations while conforming to the measures implemented by various governments.
We have, during the COVID-19 pandemic and may in the event of such similar outbreaks, have to restrict conducting our sessions online. Due to this, we may have to charge a reduced fee for our plans, while our outgoing payments may remain the same, in terms of rent and payments to Licensed Professionals and other clinical professionals employed by us. During the COVID-19, we managed to transition to the digital mode and even managed to see a larger number of enrolments due to the increased accessibility through the platform. However, we cannot assure you that we will be able to achieve such results, should there be another pandemic. Another pandemic like the COVID- 19 pandemic could, hence, affect our business, operations and financial performance.
