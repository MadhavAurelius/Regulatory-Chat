## **Registered Brokers**
Bidders can submit ASBA Forms in the Offer using the stock broker network of the Stock Exchanges,  *i.e.,*  through the Registered Brokers at the Broker Centres. The list of the Registered Brokers eligible to accept ASBA Forms,

82
including details such as postal address, telephone number and e-mail address, is provided on the websites of the respective Stock Exchanges at www.bseindia.com and www.nseindia.com, as updated from time to time.
