## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Down Syndrome (DS)** *
An extra copy of chromosome 21 causes Down Syndrome, a genetic disorder that leads to intellectual disability, characteristic physical traits, and an increased risk of health issues such as heart defects, hearing loss, and respiratory problems. Early intervention and supportive therapies empower individuals with Down Syndrome to lead fulfilling lives; despite the lifelong challenges they often face. In India, the number of down syndrome cases was estimated at approximately 0.037 million in CY20. By CY25, this figure had risen to 0.051 million, reflecting a CAGR of 6.72%. Further it is estimated to rise to 0.080 million cases by CY35, estimated to have grown at a CAGR of 4.87% for the CY25-35 period.
