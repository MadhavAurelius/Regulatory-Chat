## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Quality Control** *
We have implemented a standard operating procedure for quality assurance and feedback mechanism at our centres to ensure safety and efficacy in our programs. In this regard, we have qualified clinical professionals that oversee each step of the child’s and the family’s journey at our centres. We collect feedback by way of anonymous parent surveys and direct parent interaction. The feedback is taken on five key areas, namely, the quality of therapy, behaviour of therapists, child’s progress, centre overview, hygiene and cleanliness. Weekly and monthly reviews are done to address concerns and implement corrective actions.
