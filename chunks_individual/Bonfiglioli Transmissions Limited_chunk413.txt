## **Defaults or re-scheduling/restructuring of borrowings with financial institutions/banks**

* **Business transfer agreement dated December 15, 2025 between our Company, and Bonfiglioli Drive Solutions** * * **Private Limited** *
Pursuant to the business transfer agreement dated December 15, 2025 between our Company, and one of our Group Companies, Bonfiglioli Drive Solutions Private Limited (“  **BDSPL**  ”), our Company acquired from BDSPL the manufacturing and sale of gear components division including  *inter alia,*  all  tangible movable assets, employees, business contracts, government authorizations in relation to the business, current assets, assumed liabilities, including related business and commercial rights, title, interests etc. (“  **Business Undertaking**  ”), as a going concern by way of a slump sale, for an aggregate consideration of ₹214.40 million. This consideration was determined by way of the fair market value of the Business Undertaking pursuant to the valuation report dated December 17, 2025, prepared by Mukesh Kumar Jain, Chartered Accountants. The valuation report has been included in “  * **Material Contracts and Documents for Inspection -  Material Documents** *  ” on page 459.
Further, one of our Corporate Promoters, Bonfiglioli S.p.A., is the promoter of BDSPL and certain of our Directors, namely, Sonia Bonfiglioli, Ravindra Pisharody, Massimo Sarti, Kennady V Kaippally and Gatham Anandhan Balaji, are also directors of BDSPL.
