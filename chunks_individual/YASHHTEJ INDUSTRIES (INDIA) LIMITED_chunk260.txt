**Start**
**Two different products are extracted at this stage. i.e. Soybean crude oil and De-oiled cake (DOC)**
**Raw Material Sourcing (Soybean Seed sourced from Traders, Market Committees, Farmers and Others)**
**Management identifies potential suppliers of high- quality soybeans and evaluates them based on criteria such as price, quality, and delivery timelines. Then Purchases are made**
**Transfer of De-Oiled Cake to DOC Storage Section**
**Sample is collected for both the Soybean Crude Oil and DOC.**
**The inhouse Lab tests the sample for identifying the content of hexane present in the Crude oil. This step is undertaken to meet the industry safety standards.**
**The inhouse Lab tests the sample for identifying the content of (1) hexane (2) protein present in the DOC. This step is undertaken to meet the industry safety standards and customer requirements.**
**Crude oil and DOC is loaded in the tanks/trucks respectively belonging to the customer. Random sample of the loaded product is collected and stored in the inhouse Lab. The transportation responsibility is of the customer.**
**End**
**Based on sale orders dispatch team undertake delivery.**
**Purchased Raw Material is transported from supplier to the Factory Site. Transportation responsibility is on the supplier.**
**Weight Measurement is undertaken at Entry Gate of the Factory.**
**Random Sample Soybean Seed is Collected before unloading.**
**Sample soybean seeds are sent to the in-house laboratory for quality testing, such as moisture content, damage and foreign material. Third party testing can also be done by the supplier, if required.**
**Information is provided to the Supplier about result of Lab Report. After debit note finalisation, if any with the supplier, Raw Material received are stored in Silo.**
**Soybean Seed is sent to the Preparatory Section for further Processing.**
**Seed Cleaning: (Cleaning and removing impurities like dirt, stones, and other foreign materials)**
**Soybean Cracking/Flaking: Breaking soybeans into smaller flakes/pieces to make it easier**
**Desolventizing : (Hexane is separated from the crude oil using evaporation, leaving behind the crude soybean oil)**
