## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Acquisition of Mom’s Belief US Inc. and** *    * **Allergy & Immunology Virginia, LLC** *   **-**

By way of the Stock Purchase Agreement, our Company acquired all issued and outstanding shares of common stock of Mom's US from the Seller. The representations, warranties and indemnification obligations under the Asset Purchase Agreement were incorporated into the Stock Purchase Agreement. At closing, the aggregate consideration to be paid to the Seller for the shares was USD 1.5 million in the following manner: (a) a consideration amount of USD 0.3 million to be paid by our Company in cash, (b) Equity Shares of our Company valued at USD 0.3 million, (c) incentives subject to the terms of the Rays of Belief Limited-Stock Option Scheme 2025 by way of (i) USD 0.165 million as retention incentives, and (ii) USD 0.735 million as a performance based incentives.
The Stock Purchase Agreement includes a clause  *inter alia*  which states that in case the proposed initial public offering (  *“*   **IPO**   *”*  ) fails to occur within 36 months from the date of closing of the transaction, our Company will be required to provide liquidity for 100% of the Equity Shares held by the Seller amounting to USD 1.2 million. This liquidity option shall be in the form of a redemption, secondary sale, private placement or any other form determined by the Buyer and consented to by the Seller.
By virtue of the Rollover Agreement, the Seller contributed 2,00,000 shares constituting to 20% shares of common stock of Mom’s US to our Company, in exchange for our Company’s Equity Shares having an aggregate value of USD 0.3 million.
The valuation report for above transaction was issued on June 14, 2025, by Real Growth Securities Private Limited (Category-I Merchant Banker) for valuation of equity shares of our Company. The said valuation report has been disclosed in the section “  *Material Contracts and Documents for Inspection*  ” on page 503.
Accordingly, the transactions arising from the Asset Purchase Agreement and Stock Purchase Agreement were closed on June 23, 2025, pursuant to which, Mom’s US, became our wholly owned Subsidiary and Allergy & Immunology Virginia, LLC, became our Step-Down Subsidiary.
