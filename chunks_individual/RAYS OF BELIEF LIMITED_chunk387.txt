## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Symptoms of Speech Disorder** *
Symptoms vary based on the type of speech disorder but commonly include:
-  Difficulty forming specific sounds or words.

-  Repeated interruptions in speech flow, such as stuttering.

-  Hoarse, raspy, or strained voice.

-  Mispronunciations or substituting sounds incorrectly.

-  Challenges in organizing thoughts into coherent speech.
These symptoms may interfere with social interaction, academic performance, and overall quality of life.
