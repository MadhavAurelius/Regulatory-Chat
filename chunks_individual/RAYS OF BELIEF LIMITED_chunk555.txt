I.   *Asset Purchase Agreement dated April 18, 2025 entered into by and amongst Allergy & Immunology Virginia, LLC* *(“*   * **Buyer** *   *”), Allergy and Immunology, PLC (“*   * **Seller** *   *”) and Saju Sebastian Eapen (“*   * **Owner** *   *”) (the “*   * **Asset Purchase** * * **Agreement** *   *”) read with the Bill of Sale, Assignment and Assumption Agreement, Assignment and Assumption of* *Leases and the Interim Management Agreement, all entered into by and between the Buyer and the Seller on June 23,* *2025 (the “*   * **Ancillary Agreements** *   *”).*
By virtue of this Asset Purchase Agreement, all assets of the Seller were acquired by the Buyer, the Owner being the sole member and holding 100% of the outstanding equity interests in the Seller. As consideration for the sale and transfer to the Buyer, of the Seller’s right, title and interests in its assets, the Buyer assumed the liabilities of the Seller and paid USD 1,000 in cash by wire transfer.
The transferred assets include the Seller’s tangible and intangible properties used in its business operations, such as equipment, inventory, vehicles, supplies, contracts including customer agreements, accounts receivable, licenses, intellectual property, business records, and goodwill. Also included are prepaid expenses, software, proprietary information, and all rights related to assumed obligations, except for those explicitly excluded in the Asset Purchase Agreement.
On June 23, 2025, the Ancillary Agreements were executed by way of which the Seller’s rights, title and interest in the assets, assumed contracts, leases of the three centres in Virginia were sold, assigned, granted, conveyed and transferred, as applicable to the Buyer. The Interim Management Agreement provides for the interim operation of the centres, transferred to the Buyer, pending the transition of contracts with third-party payors. The Interim Management Agreement will terminate upon the transition of such contracts to the Buyer.
