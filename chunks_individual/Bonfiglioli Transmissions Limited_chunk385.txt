## * **Legal Metrology (National Standards) Rules, 2011** *
The Legal Metrology (National Standards) Rules, 2011 (the “  **National Standards Rules**  ”) are framed under the Metrology Act to give effect to the Metrology Act’s mandate that all weights and measures in India be based on the metric system and that reference, secondary, and working standards be specified, maintained, verified and kept traceable to national standards for legal use in trade and commerce.
