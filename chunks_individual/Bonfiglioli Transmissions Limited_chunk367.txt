## * **Agreements with BSPA; License Agreement and Intercompany Services** *

As our Promoter and significant entity of the Bonfiglioli Group, BSPA also provides services to its subsidiaries, including our Company for a fee, which is governed by an Intercompany Service Agreement dated January 1, 2024 (the “  **Intercompany Service Agreement**  ”).
Under the Intercompany Service Agreement, BSPA provides certain services to our Company (among the other entities forming part of the Bonfiglioli Group) using a combination of BSPA’s own resources as well as resources available in other companies of the Bonfiglioli Group. These services include IT, internal and external communication, administration and finance, legal and governance, human resources, quality and aftersales and other centralized services more connected with business such as product marketing, sales development, operations and purchases and similar related services. The Intercompany Service Agreement has a validity of one year and is automatically renewed for successive one-year terms unless terminated by way of a notice of at least 30 days before the year-end.
We work closely with BSPA, resulting in a number of transactions between our companies, all of which we believe take place on an arm’s length basis.
