## * **Offshore** *
Asia Pacific, led by China, is witnessing the fastest offshore build-out, deploying 8-12 MW turbines and piloting 15 MW units along rapidly developing coastal zones. North America’s offshore sector is still at an early stage but progressing, with significant projects on the US East Coast adopting 12-15 MW turbines and actively localising supply chains. Latin America’s offshore market is nascent, with early policy frameworks and feasibility studies exploring 10-14 MW turbine potential.
