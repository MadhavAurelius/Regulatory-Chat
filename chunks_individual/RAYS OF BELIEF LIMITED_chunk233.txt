* **(i) Expansion of our network of “Company Learning Centres and Company Learning Centres in partnership with** * * **Licensed Professionals”** *
We propose to utilize up to ₹ 370.87 million towards setting up 260 “  *Company Learning Centres and Company* *Learning Centres in partnership with Licensed Professionals”*  . For details on these centre model, see  *“Our Business-* *Our Centres”*  on page 235. We propose to open these centres through a strategic mix of Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities, with the flexibility to expand our service offerings in these locations in the future.
Our “  *Company Learning Centres*  ”, are independently managed centres established and operated by our Company. Our Company leases commercial spaces and undertakes full responsibility for setting up, staffing, clinical governance, marketing, and administration. These centres offer comprehensive services including consultation, assessment, goal planning, therapy, and parent training. At our  *“Company Learning Centres in partnership with Licensed* *Professionals”*  , our Company and the Licensed Professionals jointly participate in the clinical and operational delivery, forming a consolidated care team. While the Licensed Professionals bring local presence and provide referrals to our services, our Company provides our therapy services, along with standardized protocols, training, and a brand-aligned set up. Under this model, our Company and the Licensed Professional share the financial proceeds in line with mutually agreed commercial terms, which may be structured as profit-sharing or revenue percentage-based models.
The methodology for the identification and onboarding of Licensed Professionals is executed as follows:
a)  Approximately three months prior to the planned inauguration of a new centre, the Company initiates the identification process for suitable Licensed Professionals within the designated city or state. b)  This procedure involves screening, due diligence, and credential verification of potential partners to ensure their alignment with the Company’s quality and compliance standards. c)  Although the Company identifies the cities and states for expansion in advance, the formal engagement of Licensed Professionals is deferred until closer to the rollout timeline. This approach ensures that the most appropriate and currently available professionals are selected for partnership. d)  In support of its expansion strategy, the Company maintains a pipeline of potential Licensed Professionals through ongoing outreach and engagement within the industry. This pipeline allows timely identification and appointment of partners when new centres are being established.
