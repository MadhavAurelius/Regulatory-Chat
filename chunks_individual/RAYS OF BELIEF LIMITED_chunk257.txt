## **5. Inorganic Growth and General corporate purposes**

#### * **Funding Inorganic Growth through Unidentified Acquisitions** *

Our Company acquired Mom's Belief US, Inc., as our wholly owned Subsidiary and Allergy and Immunology Virginia, LLC, as our Step-Down Subsidiary in the USA, pursuant to which, we gained three centres, located in the state of Virginia to further expand our network and tap into high-quality clinical talent in the USA. These centres engage in the allergy, asthma and immunology medical practice. The clinical team includes medical doctors, registered nurses and supporting staff. The practice also caters to a paediatric population who may have overlapping developmental and behavioural needs, a portion of which, could include children with neurodivergent conditions. We intend to establish behaviour therapy support services in our three acquired centres in the USA over the next three
years. For further details on the acquisition and its impact on our financial position, please see  *“Pro Forma* *Consolidated Financial Information”, “Summary of Pro Forma Consolidated Financial Information”,* *“Management’s Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations - Pro Forma Consolidated* *Financial Information”*  and  *“History and Certain Corporate Matters - Acquisition of Mom’s Belief US, Inc. and* *Allergy & Immunology Virginia, LLC”*  on pages 353, 89, 365 and 265, respectively  *.*
We intend to utilize a portion of the Net Proceeds towards funding unidentified acquisitions in India or abroad. Some of the reasons to inorganically expand and the selection criteria that we may consider when evaluating strategic acquisitions in the future include:
-  enhance our geographical reach on a global scale;

-  existing strong team of clinical professionals;

-  growth potential of the business;

-  brand equity of the partners we may engage with; and

-  potential for ease in integration of our services.
Our acquisition strategy is primarily driven by our Board and the typical framework and process followed by us for acquisitions involves identifying the strategic acquisitions based on the rationale set out above. We conduct a detailed diligence exercise, upon the satisfactory conclusion of which, we enter into definitive agreements to acquire the target based on the approval of our Board and/or the shareholders, as required. As on the date of this Updated Draft Red Herring Prospectus -  I, we have not entered into any definitive agreements towards any future acquisitions or strategic initiatives for the object set out above.
