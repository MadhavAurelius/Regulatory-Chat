## * **Highly diversified offerings across business verticals and customer industries** *

As of September 30, 2025, under the Off-Highway Mobility Industry, we are among the few global suppliers and the largest in India whose gearbox solutions was used across propulsion, swing, and load-handling applications with a wide torque range (1,000 Nm to 3,000 kNm), enabling coverage of diverse off-highway equipment applications such as excavators, concrete mixers, mining equipment, road construction, agriculture and material handling (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). We have also developed capabilities to support our customers’ transition to electric vehicles, with offerings such as electric gearboxes and drives that is attributable to our dedicated electro- mobility team.

In the Wind Industry vertical, we have established and proven capabilities to design, manufacture, and supply fully integrated yaw and pitch drive systems for wind turbines at scale (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). We manufacture yaw and pitch drives for wind turbines that have capacities ranging from 225kw to 5mw and cater to several renewable energy companies in India and one in the United States.
Our highly diversified offerings across customer industries allows us to be well positioned to capitalize on growth across manufacturing, infrastructure and renewable sectors. It also relatively insulates us from any industry- specific slowdown.
