## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Key Outreach Pillars** *

*1.*    *Awareness and Community Engagement*
Our Company conducts its outreach principally at the community level by implementing structured awareness programs that enable caregivers and community leaders to identify early indicators of developmental challenges. These initiatives employ multilingual informational flyers and play-based parent education sessions, and maintain a presence in local paediatric clinics, playschools, and child-focused events. We typically convene these events in school or informal community settings to establish a safe forum where families can articulate their concerns and obtain professional guidance.
*2.*    *Continuing Medical Education and OPD-Based Initiatives*
We conduct CME programs for paediatricians and child health professionals, helping them identify developmental red flags early and providing frameworks for referral. These sessions are scientific, collaborative, and critical in building a community of informed practitioners. We organize outpatient department (“  **OPD**  ”) awareness camps in partnership with local hospitals and clinics to enable families to access assessments and consultations at no cost or reduced fees.

*3.*    *Digital Awareness and Ethical Outreach*
We operate our digital channels as scalable, inclusive educational platforms. We deliver live webinars, red-flag explainer videos, self-screening tools, and regional-language content to ensure that early-identification messaging reaches families across all socioeconomic strata. We disseminate content via our website, Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn in strict adherence to permission-based outreach protocols, maintaining a non-promotional tone at all times.
*4.*    *Play-Based and Peer-Led Events*
We organize interactive play-based events and workshops to provide informal and positive environments where families can observe the therapeutic impact in a non-clinical setting. These events also facilitate interactions between new families and parents who are already engaged in the therapeutic process.
We increasingly incorporate parent-led engagements into our outreach strategy. Through these platforms, they share their lived experiences and offer encouragement to families who are in the initial stages of the process  * **.** *
