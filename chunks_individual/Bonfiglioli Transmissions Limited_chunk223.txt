## **Special indirect tax benefits available to the Company**

o  With payment of IGST. Under this scenario, the exporter is allowed to claim refund of IGST paid on exports.
The company is undertaking export of goods with payment of integrated tax and claim refund of IGST paid on exports. The company also undertakes export of goods without payment of integrated tax under LUT for which refund is not applied.
-  The company is availing the benefit of making deferred payment of import duty on a fortnightly basis, which

 is a trade facilitation measure governed by the Deferred Payment of Import Duty Rules, 2016.
-  Preferential duty benefits are available under bilateral and multilateral Foreign Trade Agreements (FTA)

 entered by India with countries like Vietnam, Japan, Thailand etc. on import of goods into India. These benefits are implemented through the Customs Act, 1962 and the notifications issued thereunder, including Notification No. 69/2011-Customs dated 29.07.2011 (for Japan), Notification No. 46/2011-Customs dated 01.06.2011 (for ASEAN, subject to the fulfilment of prescribed conditions.
