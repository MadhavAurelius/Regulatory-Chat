* **Business transfer agreement dated September 30, 2024 between our Company and Bonfiglioli Technology** * * **Space Private Limited, read with the addendum to the business transfer agreement dated October 23, 2024** *
Pursuant to the business transfer agreement dated September 30, 2024 between our Company, and one of our Group Companies, Bonfiglioli Technology Space Private Limited (“  **BTSPL**  ”), our Company sold its global capability center business providing engineering design services, IT support services and accounting, finance and consolidation services  including  *inter alia,*  all tangible movable assets, employees, business contracts, current

assets, government authorizations in relation to the business, assumed liabilities, including related business and commercial rights, title, interests etc. to BTSPL , as a going concern by way of a slump sale, for an aggregate consideration of ₹115.50 million. This consideration was determined using a cost approach -  replacement cost method and in accordance with the valuation report dated September 30, 2024, prepared by S S P Jain and Associates LLP, Chartered Accountants. The valuation report has been included in “  * **Material Contracts and** * * **Documents for Inspection -  Material Documents** *  ” on page 459.
