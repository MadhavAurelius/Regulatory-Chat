## **6. Social Skills Training**
-   **Social Skills Groups**  : Often used for individuals with ASD or ADHD to improve their ability

 to interact appropriately with peers, make friends, and navigate social situations.

-   **Social Stories**  : A therapeutic technique used primarily for children with ASD, these stories

 describe social situations and appropriate responses, helping children learn social norms and reduce anxiety in social interactions.
