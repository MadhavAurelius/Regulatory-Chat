## * **Global off-Highway Mobility Solutions (Drives) -  Market Size and Forecast** *
Global Off-Highway market size for Mobility solutions (Drives), (Value in USD Bn), Calendar Year 2024 - Calendar Year 2030E

Demand for Mobility Solutions (Drives) used in Off-Highway applications is driven by High equipment utilization in core sectors (mining, construction, roads, agriculture), strong replacement cycles and OEM demand for reliable, rugged, high-torque drivetrain systems. The future growth is driven by the electrification of off-highway equipment, demand for energy-efficient and digitally monitored drives, the expansion of infrastructure and mining projects, and the OEM push towards integrated drive systems.
