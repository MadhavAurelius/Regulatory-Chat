Agriculture Equipment (Tractors, Harvesters, Sprayers)

Road Construction Machinery (Pavers, Compactors, Rollers)
National highway expansion, city road upgrades, resurfacing cycles, runway works, and fleet modernization drive steady demand for paving and compaction drives. The government has identified 434 projects under the PM Gati Shakti initiative with an outlay of Rs. 11.17 lakh crore to improve multimodal connectivity and logistics infrastructure.

Concrete & Mixer Equipment (Transit Mixers, Self-Loading Mixers)
Urban real estate, metro, and industrial construction push strong demand for mixer drives as RMC usage expands across Tier 1- 3 cities. Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY-U 2.0), the Government has approved a ₹10 lakh crore investment for urban housing, with ₹2.30 lakh crore of direct government assistance over five years.
