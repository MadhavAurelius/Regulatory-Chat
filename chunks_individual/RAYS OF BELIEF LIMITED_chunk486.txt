## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Awards and Recognitions** *

Our Company has received prestigious recognitions for its work in supporting children with developmental challenges, including, the “  *Zero Project Award*  ” conferred at the United Nations in Vienna, Austria in 2019. We were recognized as one of the innovative practices by the Zero Project’s selection committee of renowned disability and accessibility experts for being exemplary in the areas of innovation, impact, chances of long-term growth and success, and scalability. We were recognised for our empowerment of parents and clinical professionals who support special needs kids and were regarded as outstanding in providing a practical solution to improve accessibility for persons with disabilities.
Further, we were honoured by The Economic Times as one of the “  *Promising Brands*  ” of 2019. Our Company also received the prestigious “  *International Business Excellence Award*  ” in 2019, specifically in the wellbeing and health category. In 2020, the Times of India recognised our brand “Mom’s Belief” as one of the pre-eminent mother and child brands.
Additionally, our founder, Promoter, and Managing Director, Nitin Bindlish, was featured in the “  *BW 40 under 40 list* *and Social Entrepreneur*  ” of the year 2020, which was facilitated to him for his great endeavours and achievements.
