## **Luciano Bonfiglioli**
Luciano Bonfiglioli, born on May 3, 1966, aged 59 years, is one of the Individual Promoters of our Company. He resides at Via Degli Scalini, N 19, Bologna, Italy. He holds a degree of doctor of economics and commerce from the University of Bologna. He has experience in the business of manufacturing mechanical components and was previously associated with Bonfiglioli S.p.A. (  *formerly Bonfiglioli Riduttori S.p.A*  ) as an industrial manager. He is also currently on the board of Bonfiglioli S.p.A.
