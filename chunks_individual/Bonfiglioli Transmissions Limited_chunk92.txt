* **27.** *    * **We require power, fuel and water for our operations and any disruption to the supply of power, fuel** * * **or water could disrupt our manufacturing operations and increase our production costs, which could** * * **adversely affect our results of operations.** *
Adequate and uninterrupted supply of electrical power and water is critical to our operations. Although we maintain power back-ups through generators at each of our manufacturing facilities, we may experience disruptions in power supply due to natural calamities or scarcity of fuel supply at our manufacturing facilities from time to time. Any such disruption may lead us to use our back-up generators and prolonged use of such generators may increase our production costs. Significant and prolonged disruptions in the availability of power and fuel could require us to suspend our operations. We may also be affected by an increase in power and fuel costs that we may not be able to adequately pass on to our customers. Any significant increase in the cost of electricity and fuel could lead to an increase in production cost and adversely affect our profitability and operating margins. Operations at our manufacturing facilities also depend on a steady supply of water. While we have not faced disruptions in supply of water at our manufacturing facilities in the nine months period ended September 30, 2025 and Fiscals 2024, 2023 and 2022, an insufficient supply of water any time in the future may result in a need to limit or delay or suspend our operations, which could adversely affect our business, financial condition, results of operations, and cash flows.
