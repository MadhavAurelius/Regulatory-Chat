## **Insurance**
We maintain insurance policies covering risks such as fire, natural disasters such as cyclones, earthquakes, burglary, theft, external accidental damage, terrorism, breakdowns (electronic or mechanical) of electronics, as required by applicable law and to protect our assets and operations and also in respect of our employees such as health and life insurance. Our insurance coverage is procured from third-party insurers, with policy limits and terms based on value/ cost of assets insured, consignment value, safeguards or security measures currently implemented by us. While our current insurance coverage is adequate to address ordinary and foreseeable risks, certain risks may not be fully insured or insurable.
