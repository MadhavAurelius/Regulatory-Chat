## **DIVIDEND POLICY**

The declaration and payment of dividend on the Equity Shares, if any, will be recommended by our Board and approved by our Shareholders, at their discretion subject to the provisions of the Articles of Association and applicable law, including the Companies Act, read with the applicable rules issued thereunder, each as amended and the dividend distribution policy of our Company.
The dividend distribution policy of our Company was approved and adopted by our Board on February 5, 2026 (“  **Dividend Policy**  ”). In terms of the Dividend Policy, the dividend, if any, will depend on a number of internal and external factors, which includes,  *inter alia*  , (i) internal factors such as net profits earned during the financial year, accumulated reserves including retained earnings, mandatory transfer of profits earned to specific reserves, past dividend trends, liquidity and return ratios, future capital expenditure of our Company, current and projected cash balance and our Company’s working capital requirements, any other significate developments that require cash investments; any (ii) external factors such as any significant changes in the macro-economic environment, any political, tax and regulatory changes, any change in the business or technological environment resulting in our Company making significant investment or any changes in the competitive environment resulting in a requirement for significant investment.
Any future determination as to the declaration and payment of dividends will be at the discretion of our Board and Shareholders. The dividend, if any, will depend on the aforementioned parameters and on factors that our Board deems relevant.
