## **Procurement and Suppliers**

We maintain a well-diversified base of domestic suppliers across India to support our localisation strategy. As of September 30, 2025 we had a network of 255 suppliers of which 186 were located in India and 69 were located overseas including in Italy, China, USA and Germany.

We depend on a combination of Indian and global suppliers, including our Group Companies, to provide components such as iron and aluminum casting, forging, gears, bearings, seals, grease and oils, for assembling our gearboxes. These suppliers in turn source their own supplies such as steel, iron, aluminum, plastic, petroleum products from domestic and global suppliers.
We place purchase orders for our monthly supply requirements at least 1.5 months in advance, with the ability to amend orders with at least 2 weeks’ notice. The unit price of the parts is determined based on negotiations between both parties. Our supplier selection process is based on a wide variety of factors, including technical expertise, product quality, location, and financial stability. We typically enter into a basic purchase agreement with suppliers under which we agree to (i) share with the suppliers the specifications of the ordered parts including documents, drawings and samples as certified by our Company, (ii) supply raw materials, semi-finished products wherever applicable to the supplier in relation to the ordered parts and (iii) supply certain special tools such as fixtures, cutters, broaches, casting and forging tools, in relation to the ordered parts to the suppliers.
