## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Focus on accessibility** *

*Accessibility to convenient and affordable developmental care:*
Our centres are strategically located across Tier 1, Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities to ensure equitable access to therapies and care for affected families and children. Some of our centres are also set up in schools, where students of these schools in need of our services can enrol and avail them. These locations support easy, repeated visits which are critical for sustained therapy programs. We also provide our services through digital mode to ensure that those who are unable to physically visit our centres can also access and benefit from our services.
During the COVID-19 pandemic, we offered comprehensive online programs and e-therapy sessions for free or on a pay-as-you-like basis for the lockdown period. In February 2022, we launched an insurance plan, underwritten by a healthcare insurance provider, for children enrolled with us. This insurance covers hospitalisation, online, offline and home therapies and OPD costs incurred for pre-existing conditions through cashless benefits and settling reimbursement claims. As of September 30, 2025, families of 429 children enrolled with us had availed this insurance, with 47 of them having active policies.
