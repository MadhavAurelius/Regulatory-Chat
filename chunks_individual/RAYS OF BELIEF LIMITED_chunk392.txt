#### **Available treatments for Speech Disorder**
Speech disorders are managed based on type, severity, and cause. Primary treatments include speech therapy, where Speech-Language Pathologists work on articulation, fluency, voice, and language skills. Assistive tools like speech- generating devices or apps support communication for severe cases.
-   **Speech Therapy:**  Focuses on improving articulation, fluency, voice, and language.

-   **Assistive Tools:**  Devices like speech-generating systems or mobile apps for severe cases.

-   **Medications:**  Addresses underlying conditions like neurological issues or anxiety.

-   **Surgical Interventions:**  For physical abnormalities like cleft palate.

-   **Psychological Support:**  Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) for emotional challenges.
