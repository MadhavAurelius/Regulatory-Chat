We intend to strengthen our Industry & Automation vertical by deepening our presence in end-user industries where capacity expansion and modernisation are expected to remain robust between Fiscal 2025 and Fiscal 2030, including cement, metals and steel, plastics and polymers, rubber and tyres, ports and shipbuilding, defence and heavy engineering, sugar and power generation (  *Source: F&S* *Report*  ). India’s industrial and automation solutions market is expected to grow on the back of a manufacturing upcycle, localisation and energy-efficiency mandates, with users shifting towards more efficient, digitally enabled gearboxes and gear motors (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). We plan to address this opportunity by expanding our portfolio of application-engineered and integrated electro-mechanical drive solutions to increase our share of new projects, upgrades and maintenance cycles. We intend to further develop our Off-Highway Mobility Solutions vertical by focusing on equipment segments that are expected to benefit from sustained growth in India’s infrastructure, mining, agriculture, housing and logistics sectors (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). India’s off-highway drive market is expected to enter a technology upgrade and localisation cycle, with original equipment manufacturers shifting to more efficient, digitally optimised and locally sourced drive architectures (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). We plan to capitalise on this by collaborating with OEMs on new platforms, widening our range of locally engineered drive modules and strengthening our aftermarket presence through service, spares and retrofit solutions. Global wind market growth is supported by widespread repowering in mature markets, rapid scale-up in Asia, and stronger hybrid and offshore pipelines globally. Growth in India’s wind sector is being driven by strong SECI auctions, hybrid and round-the-clock procurements, and supportive state policies  *(Source: F&S Report)*  . We intend to capitalise on the growing demand by introducing solutions to meet evolving needs of wind OEMs to maintain our leadership position in India. * **Broaden product portfolio and accelerate new product launches** * We intend to broaden our product portfolio to target market opportunities and address product gaps. We also propose to strengthen our market position in select industries within Industry & Automation where we have an existing presence. For instance, while we are a leading manufacturer of low-medium duty gear motors for Industry & Automation customers and have been manufacturing these gear motors since 2000, we strive to strengthen our presence in heavy-duty gearboxes as well. We propose to achieve this by leveraging our brand recognition, the Bonfiglioli Group’s R&D infrastructure, our local manufacturing and testing capabilities, and existing customer relationships to supply heavy duty gearboxes to existing customers of low to medium duty gearboxes. The demand for heavy-duty gearboxes is expected to rise, driven by OEMs that manufacture machinery for industries such as steel, cement, sugar, rubber, plastics, and power generation, and has been reflected in the interest for heavy-duty gearboxes from customers of our low to medium duty gearboxes. As a result the estimated market size for heavy duty gearboxes is projected to be ₹55,200 million by Fiscal 2030 (  *Source: F&S Report*  ). To meet this demand, we propose to set up a dedicated manufacturing facility in Talegaon (Maharashtra) that will focus on manufacturing heavy-duty gearboxes.
