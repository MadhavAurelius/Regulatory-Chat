## **Macro-economic Trends of the Global Economy and Outlook of Global GDP Trends**
The global economy is expected to grow by 3.0% in Calendar Year 2025F and 3.1% in Calendar Year 2026F. Economic activity remains broad-based, with contributions from domestic consumption, services, and investment in several large emerging markets. From 2025 to 2029, global nominal GDP is projected to reach approximately USD 140 trillion, with real GDP growth averaging 3.2% annually. While legal challenges to the tariff regime create uncertainty, the Calendar Year 2025E growth forecast has inched up to 3.0%, aided by pre-tariff front- loading.
