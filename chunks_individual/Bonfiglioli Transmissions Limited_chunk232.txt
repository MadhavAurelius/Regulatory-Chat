## * **GDP Breakdown by Key Sectors** *
*Manufacturing*
India’s manufacturing sector has accounted for 17%-18% of the total Gross Value Added (GVA) over the past decade. Over the medium term, India’s manufacturing share is expected to rise from its current ~17% of GDP to 20- 21% by Fiscal 2030, supported by PLI schemes across 14+ sectors and an outlay of ₹2.13 trillion, including pharmaceuticals, textiles, medical devices, auto components, and white goods.

*India -  Preferred Destination for Global Manufacturing Hub*
The National Manufacturing Mission was announced in the Union Budget for Fiscal 2025- 26 to strengthen Atmanirbhar Bharat. Key initiatives include GST implementation, corporate tax reduction, FDI liberalization, and extensive measures to ease doing business.
