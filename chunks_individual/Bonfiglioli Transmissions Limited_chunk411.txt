**Details regarding material acquisitions or divestments of business/undertakings, mergers, amalgamations, any revaluation of assets, etc., in the last 10 years**
Except as disclosed below, our Company has not made any material acquisitions or divestments of business/undertakings, mergers, amalgamation, any revaluation of assets, etc., in the last 10 years:
* **The Scheme of Arrangement for Demerger amongst Bonfiglioli Renewable Power Conversion India Private** * * **Limited (“BRPCI”), our Company and their respective shareholders (“Scheme of Arrangement for** * * **Demerger”)** *
Pursuant to a Scheme of Arrangement for Demerger, as approved by our Board by way of its resolution dated January 17, 2017 and the NCLT, Chennai by way of its order dated October 25, 2017 (“  **NCLT Order**  ”), the businesses of BRPCI (“  **Demerged Entity**  ”), including manufacturing of solar inverters for photovoltaic applications, and system integration and testing for control and relay panels, gas insulated sub-station panels, automation panels, automated power factor correction panels and gas analyser panels and includes all assets and properties, contracts, intellectual property, regulatory licenses and approvals, proceedings, employees, and all related liabilities and obligations in respect thereof (“  **Solar Business Undertaking**  ”) demerged from the Demerged Entity and vested in our Company on a going-concern basis. The appointed date for the Scheme of Arrangement for Demerger was April 1, 2016, and the effective date was December 11, 2017, i.e., the date of filing the NCLT Order with the RoC.
As consideration for the transfer of the Solar Business Undertaking, our Company agreed to issue and allot 1 Equity Share of face value ₹10 each to the equity shareholder of BRPCI, i.e., Bonfiglioli Vectron GmbH, for every 11 fully paid-up equity shares of face value ₹10 each held by it in BRPCI, determined by conducting a relative valuation of our Company and the Solar Business Undertaking in accordance with generally accepted professional standards, pursuant to an independent valuation report dated January 9, 2017, prepared by chartered accountants N.C Rajagopal & Co., using (i) the asset approach; and (ii) the discounted cash flow approach. The valuation report has been included in “  * **Material Contracts and Documents for Inspection -  Material Documents** *  ” on page 459.
