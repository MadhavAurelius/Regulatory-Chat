## **Sales and Distribution**

We manufacture and supply products for OEMs based in India as well as outside India. Most of our I&A customers are based in India, with a few Industry & Automation customers in China, Germany, Brazil, Australia. Majority of our customers under Off-Highway Mobility Industry and Wind Industry verticals are also located in India, while our largest customer for Wind Industry is located in the United States.
