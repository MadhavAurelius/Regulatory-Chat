## * **Off-Highway Mobility Industry** *

One of our primary challenges in co-engineering with the Off-Highway OEM was developing gearboxes tailored to the specific conditions found in India, while ensuring they remained competitive at market-driven price points. We encountered unique demands arising from intensive duty cycles and harsh environmental factors. To address these, we applied our engineering expertise to map operational loads and analyse real-world site conditions directly in the field. These investigations informed the design of entirely new drives featuring heavy-duty gears, bearings with increased load capacity, and robust sealing systems. We proceeded to test and manufacture these components to precisely address the Off-Highway OEM’s requirements.
