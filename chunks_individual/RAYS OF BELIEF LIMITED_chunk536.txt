#### **State Law related to the Regulation of the Practice of Medicine:**
Virginia Board of Medicine Regulations and Virginia Medical Practice Act provide  comprehensive medical practice regulations covering physician licensure, scope of practice, continuing education, professional conduct and patient care standards, and disciplinary procedures. The practice of medicine in most states is defined as actions involving clinical prevention, diagnosis, or treatment of human diseases, injuries, or conditions.
