#### * **Consumer Price Index** *
The Consumer Price Index (CPI) for April- December 2025 recorded a combined inflation rate of 1.7%, there was an increase of 62 basis points in December 2025 from November 2025 in headline inflation. The increase in headline
inflation in December 2025 was driven by increase in inflation of personal care and effects, vegetables, meat and fish, egg, spices and pulses.
