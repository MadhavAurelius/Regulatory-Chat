## **Manufacturing Facilities**

*Thirumudivakkam Unit*
We own and operate our facility in Thirumudivakkam, which has been operational since 1999, and is spread across 11.63 acres. The power requirements of the facility are met through the state grid and DG power sets. As of September 30, 2025, the facility held certifications including ISO 9001 (Quality Management System), ISO 14001 (Environment Management System) and ISO 45001 (Occupational Health and Safety).

*Talegaon Unit*
Our facility in Pune was initially located at Chakkan and has been operational since 2017. In 2023, the facility was relocated to Talegaon and is spread across 10.50 acres. The power requirements of the facility are met through the state grid and DG power sets. As of September 30, 2025, the facility held certifications including ISO 9001 (Quality Management System), ISO 14001 (Environment Management System) and ISO 45001 (Occupational Health and Safety).
