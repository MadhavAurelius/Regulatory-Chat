## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Phase 1: Site Identification, Evaluation and Finalisation** *

We initiate our site identification process by evaluating locations that exhibit alignment with our core demographic i.e., families with children requiring developmental support. This process is conducted by our Business Development team, who identify locations based on the following indicative parameters:
i.  Their proximity to paediatric hospitals, child neurologists, developmental paediatricians, allied health professionals etc, to ensure access to organic referral networks, collaborations with medical practitioners, and helps enhance credibility through association with established healthcare providers.
ii.  Areas with a concentration of playschools, daycares, and preschools.
iii.  Sites with adequate parking, public transportation access, and overall convenience for families with children.
iv.  Sites allowing for sufficient frontage, signage placement, and brand expression.
Once potential sites are shortlisted, we evaluate the new location on whether it is accessible to our targeted demographic, whether we would be addressing service gaps in that area and whether we would get referral opportunities. We also assess the feasibility of the rental terms of the properties we may take on lease for setting up the new centre, along with a study of whether the layout of the property would be suitable for our centres.
If a site satisfies these aspects, we proceed to the final stage of lease negotiations.
