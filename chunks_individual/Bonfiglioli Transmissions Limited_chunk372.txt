## **Product and Customer Service**
*After Sales Service*
We provide quality customer services through two channels: (i) our in-house team of engineers that are directly tasked with service support at our manufacturing facilities and sales offices, and (ii) authorized third-party service facilities. The table below sets forth details of our after-sales service touchpoints as of September 30, 2025:
**Particulars Number** In-house service facilities (including manufacturing facilities and sales offices)
13
In-house service engineers 15 Authorized third-party service facilities 13 Service engineers at authorized third-party service facilities 26

Our average field-response time is within the next working day, which helps maximize product uptime and lifecycle value.
*Warranty*
We offer standard warranties to our third-party customers which differs based on the end-use/ vertical we cater to. Products under our Industry & Automation vertical typically have a warranty of 12 months from the date of commissioning or 18 months from the date of invoice, while those under the Off-Highway Mobility and Wind Industry verticals have a warranty period of around 24 months.
