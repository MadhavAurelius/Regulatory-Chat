We benefit significantly by being a part of the Bonfiglioli Group, which enables us to leverage its global customer relationships and supply products and solutions to the Indian businesses of international players. With access to the extensive research and development expertise of BSPA, including a broad portfolio of innovative products, we are able to meet the localized requirements of our customers. As of September 30, 2025, the Bonfiglioli Group had seven R&D centers globally including in India, Italy, Germany and China, that employed 377 employees.
We regularly liaise with the R&D and IT center located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India (“  **India R&D Centre**  ”) which is housed under Bonfiglioli Technology Space Private Limited (“  **BTSPL**  ”), a company forming part of the Bonfiglioli Group. The India R&D Centre has test facilities for products across all three of our business verticals, and as of September 30, 2025, it was the only test laboratory in India to test yaw and pitch drives for wind turbines (  *Source: F&S Report*  ).  The proximity of our manufacturing operations with Bonfiglioli Group’s global R&D ecosystem enables faster product development through strong design manufacturing alignment and early supplier involvement, resulting in faster time-to-market and enhancing our responsiveness to India-specific customer requirements, establishing our position as a specialised solutions provider in India. The India R&D Centre is the only R&D centre in the Bonfiglioli Group that caters to all three end-user verticals, which allows us to leverage its development capabilities across our entire portfolio of products and services.
