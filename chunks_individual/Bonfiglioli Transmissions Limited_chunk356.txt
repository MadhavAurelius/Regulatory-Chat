*Products Co-Engineered with Customers*
We deliver bespoke, co-engineered products to our customers under the vertical where the intellectual property for the product remains with the Bonfiglioli Group. To this end, we undertake requirement analysis, application analysis, product feasibility, virtual validation, prototyping and testing, field validation and serial production. This process can take up to 30 months, leading to long-term associations with our customers, as illustrated by a few case studies below.

*Co-Engineering - Prominent Construction Equipment Supplier in India (“Off-Highway OEM”)*
Our co-engineering process follows a sequence of well-defined stages that are personalized to the Off-Highway OEM’s technical and operational requirements. We begin with a three-month requirements analysis, which captures performance targets, reliability criteria, and operational data specific to the intended application. This is followed by application analysis and product feasibility assessments over six months, during which we evaluate potential concepts, manufacturability, and serviceability. Virtual validation is used to assess gear strength and fatigue life for two months, leading into four months of prototype assembly and endurance testing under controlled conditions. Field validation comes next, where gearboxes are tested in real-world environments for about a year. This entire process can take around 25 months. Following careful review of the test data, the final product moves into serial production.
