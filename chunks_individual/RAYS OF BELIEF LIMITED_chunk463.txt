## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### * **Continued expansion of our network with a focus on driving cost efficiencies across our operations** *

We intend to continue to expand our geographic footprint and deepen client reach in India, primarily through our organic expansion strategy, by setting up centres in new cities and towns and strengthening our position in existing cities and towns through our centre rollout model. For further details, please see “  *Our Business - New Centre Roll Out* *Process*  ” on page 239. We have a Business Development team comprising of 36 employees who continuously monitor our target markets for expansion. Our key criteria for evaluating and selecting potential locations and cities for expansion includes proximity to healthcare centres and doctor clinics, daycares, and preschools.
We believe that we are well positioned to benefit from such trends due to our existing network of centres spread across 7 Tier 1, 35 Tier 2 and 15 Tier 3 cities as well as the strategic advantage enjoyed from our early focus on Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities. We intend to continue building our presence in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities to target the large, underserved communities in these regions which lack access to adequate developmental care facilities for NDDs.
Since all our centres are opened on lease basis, our business model allows us to roll out new centres with optimal upfront investment, limited capital expenditure and high scalability. As we scale our business, we intend to continue focusing on such a model for procuring centre space and managing our administrative expenses to improve our operational and cost efficiency and profitability.
We intend to utilize ₹576.20 million of the proceeds raised from the Issue for setting up 414 new centres between Fiscal 2027 and 2029. For details on the use of proceeds of the Issue for setting up of new centres, please see “  *Objects* *of the Issue*  ” on page 115.
As part of our growth strategy, we also intend to expand our domestic as well as international presence through inorganic growth opportunities such as acquisitions and strategic alliances such as our  *“Company Learning Centres* *in partnership with Licensed Professionals”*  model.
On an international level, our Company acquired Mom's Belief US, Inc., as our wholly owned Subsidiary and Allergy and Immunology Virginia, LLC, as our Step-Down Subsidiary in the USA, pursuant to which, we gained three centres, located in the state of Virginia to further expand our network and tap into high-quality clinical talent in the USA. These centres engage in the allergy, asthma and immunology medical practice. The clinical team includes medical doctors, registered nurses and supporting staff. The practice also caters to a paediatric population who may have overlapping developmental and behavioural needs, a portion of which, could include children with neurodivergent conditions.
