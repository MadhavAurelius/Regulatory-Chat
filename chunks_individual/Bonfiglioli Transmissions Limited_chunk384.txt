## * **Legal Metrology Act, 2009 and the Legal Metrology (General) Rules, 2011** *
The Legal Metrology Act, 2009, (the “  **Metrology Act**  ”) aims to establish and enforce standards of weights and measures, regulate trade and commerce in weights, measures and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto. Any transaction/contract relating to goods/class of goods or undertakings shall be as per the weight/measurement/numbers prescribed by the Metrology Act. The regulations and specifications with respect to the exact denomination of the weight or measure or number of goods to be considered in transactions are contained in the Legal Metrology (General) Rules, 2011 made by the Central Government under the Metrology Act.
