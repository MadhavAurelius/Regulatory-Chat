## * **India off-Highway Mobility Solutions (Drives) Market -  New and Replacement Demand** *
India’s drives market remains predominantly driven by new equipment additions (90%), reflecting strong capex activity across infrastructure-linked sectors. Replacement demand is structurally lower today (10%) but is expected to rise as utilization increases and the installed base matures. However, ongoing OEM focus on higher durability and lifecycle optimization is likely to temper replacement intensity over time.
