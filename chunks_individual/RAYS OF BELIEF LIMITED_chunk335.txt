## **2. Speech and Language Therapy**
-   **Speech Therapy**  : Often used for conditions like ASD, Down syndrome, intellectual disability,

 and communication disorders to improve language development, articulation, and social communication skills.
-   **Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC)**  : For individuals who cannot speak

 or have limited verbal communication abilities, AAC systems (e.g., communication boards or electronic devices) are used to assist in expression.
