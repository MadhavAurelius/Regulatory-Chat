## * **Aftermarket opportunity:** *
The global gearbox aftermarket offers a strong recurring revenue stream driven by a massive installed base and continuous replacement, repair, and refurbishment needs. Industries such as cement, mining, steel, wind, and logistics rely heavily on service uptime, creating steady demand for spares, rebuilds, and efficiency upgrades. Digital diagnostics, condition monitoring, and predictive maintenance are emerging as high-value add-ons. OEMs that combine faster service response, reliable spares, and upgrade kits are best positioned to capture this growing aftermarket opportunity. At a global level, aftermarket (spares, service, refurbishment, upgrades) contributes 20- 25% of total gearbox industry revenues.
