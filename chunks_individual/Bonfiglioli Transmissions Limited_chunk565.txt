## **Expenses**

Expenses comprise cost of raw materials consumed, changes in inventories of finished goods and work-in- progress, employee benefits expense, finance costs, depreciation and amortisation expense, and other expenses.
*Cost of raw materials & components consumed.*  Cost of raw materials & components consumed comprises the cost of raw materials and components we use in our manufacturing operations, such as iron and aluminium castings, forgings, gears, bearings.
*Changes in inventories of finished goods and work-in-progress.*  Changes in inventories of finished goods and work-in-progress comprises opening stock and closing stock, which each comprise net increase or decrease in stock of work-in-progress and finished goods.
*Employee benefits expense.*  Employee benefits expense comprises salaries, wages and bonus, and contribution to provident and gratuity, and staff welfare expenses. Subsequent to the nine-months period ended September 30, 2025, The Ministry of Labour & Employment issued a notification stating that the Code on Wages, 2019; the Industrial Relations Code, 2020; the Code on Social Security, 2020; and the Occupational Safety, Health and Working Conditions Code, 2020 will be effective from November 21, 2025. Our Company has assessed that the enactment of these Labour Codes and their resulting impact represent a non-adjusting event for the financial statements for the nine-months period ended September 30, 2025, in accordance with Ind AS 10 -  Events after the Reporting Period. Furthermore, the financial effect is not determined in the financial statements for the nine- months period ended September 2025 since our Company is in the process of carrying out a detailed evaluation

of the financial implications arising from the implementation of the Labour Codes and expects to recognize a provision for the consequential impact in its financial statements for the year ended December 31, 2025
