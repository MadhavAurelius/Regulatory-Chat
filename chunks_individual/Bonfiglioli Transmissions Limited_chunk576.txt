## * **Fiscal 2024 compared to Fiscal 2023** *

Employee benefits expense (continuing operations). Employee benefits expense (continuing operations) increased by 9.60% from ₹895.93 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹981.94 million in Fiscal 2024, primarily due to an increase in salaries, wages and bonus that increased by 8.25% from ₹784.66 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹849.37 million in Fiscal 2024, as a result of annual increments in salaries.
Depreciation and amortisation expense (continuing operations) . Depreciation and amortisation expense increased by 24.02% from ₹363.07 million for Fiscal 2023 to ₹450.27 million for Fiscal 2024, primarily due to an increase in depreciation of property, plant and equipment from ₹311.45 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹405.65 million in Fiscal 2024, which was due to establishment of the new assembly facility at Talegaon, Pune in the fourth quarter of 2023.
Finance costs (continuing operations) . Finance costs decreased by 20.10% from ₹130.48 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹104.25 million in Fiscal 2024, primarily due to a decrease in interest on borrowings from ₹77.58 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹30.54 million in Fiscal 2024, as we did not avail any borrowings from banks post July 2024. This was partially offset by an increase in other borrowing costs which increased from ₹47.57 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹66.77 million in Fiscal 2024.
Other expenses (continuing operations) . Other expenses (continuing operations) increased by 3.91% from ₹2,694.30 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹2,799.66 million in Fiscal 2024. This increase was primarily attributable to:
-  Higher royalty, which increased by 9.27% from ₹752.35 million in Fiscal 2023 to ₹822.09 million in Fiscal

 2024, as a result of higher sales.
-  Power and fuel expenses (continuing operations), which increased by 19.50% from ₹176.19 million in Fiscal

 2023 to ₹210.55 million in Fiscal 2024, mainly due to an increase in production to meet the increase in sales/ orders placed, and partly due to an increase in the per unit cost of electricity as prices were hiked by the state electricity board of Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu.
-  Allowance for doubtful debts which increased from no such allowance in Fiscal 2023 to ₹23.24 million in

 Fiscal 2024, as a result of non-payment of dues by one of our OEM customers in Fiscal 2024.
