## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### **Main Objects of our Company**

The main objects of our Company contained in its Memorandum of Association are as disclosed below:
*1.*    *To empower every parent (India and abroad) both of normal and that of autistic and Children with special need* *so that their child can develop skills through specific resources under their guidance. Its aim is to enable* *mother/parents with tools that can help their child to develop or further enhance the developmental skills. The* *child does not have to be dependent on time bound classes or time based development opportunities. This will* *be provided by parents/guide at home for the comfort of child.*
*2.*    *To contribute to improvements to the well-being of children particularly those with special needs, as well as to* *the reduction of vulnerabilities to situations and actions that lead to discrimination, neglect, abuse, exploitation* *of child and to abandonment and separation of children by society.*
*3.*    *To be a centre for capacity building; collection, collation and dissemination of information on all aspects of* *protection and empowerment of children and act as a forum of advocacy for the formulation and implementation* *of child development and training policies, plans and programs.*
*4.*    *To open various child development centres across India and abroad that help, guide, coach and train aid normal* *and special needs children in their individual development.*
*5.*    *To identify and work on gifted children of all age group and provide various tools/resources to tap their talent* *and help them to achieve their full potential.*
*6.*    *To leverage the efforts in India & abroad of various departments/organizations, universities, educational* *institute, companies, research organization, government, NGOs, civil societies and international development* *agencies for effective implementation of the programs and services which are aimed at protection and* *empowerment of women, children and disabled persons.*
*7.*    *To design and commission for normal and special needs children Research and Development centres with main* *objective to enhance quality of developmental tools/resources to identify and improve.*
*8.*    *To maintain professional, transparent and ethical management culture in all its activities/ bodies etc.*
*9.*    *To initiate subscription-based information to perspective parents, schools & other organizations.*
