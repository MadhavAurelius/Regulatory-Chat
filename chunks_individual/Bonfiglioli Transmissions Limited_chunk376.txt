Our workforce receives comprehensive support through structured induction training, on-the-job guidance, and a wide range of technical, functional, and behavioural development programs aimed at enhancing employee capabilities. We have a buddy system in place, where every new joiner is supported by an existing employee who acts as a buddy to help them settle smoothly into the organization. We conduct structured interventions with new employees at different stages of their journey, including at 90 and 180 days, along with interaction sessions with top management.
Function- and level-specific training interventions are designed to support employees’ self-development and enable them to develop their team members in achieving common organizational goals. Our training programs also include safety procedures, technical and functional programs, as well as managerial effectiveness and leadership development initiatives across the organization. In addition, we have established employee benefit programs and people-centric policies that promote a positive workplace culture, diversity, and inclusion.
