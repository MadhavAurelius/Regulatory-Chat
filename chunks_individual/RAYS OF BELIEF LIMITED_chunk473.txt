## **5. Support Groups and Coaching**

#### **Our Centres**

“  *Our centres in India*  ”
*Note: Map not to scale*

We provide our services across India through our following centres:
*1.*    *Company Learning Centres*
These are independently managed centres established and operated by our Company. The operational structure and revenue model for these types of centres are as follows:
i.  Operational Structure:
Our Company leases commercial spaces and undertakes full responsibility for setting up, staffing, clinical governance, marketing, and administration. These centres offer comprehensive services including consultation, assessment, goal planning, therapy, and parent training.
ii.  Revenue Model:
All service revenues generated are retained by our Company, which enables direct profitability and control over cost structures, quality, and brand experience.
*2.*    *Company Learning Centres in partnership with Licensed Professionals*
These centres are operated in collaboration with paediatricians, developmental paediatricians, child psychiatrists, occupational therapists, speech-language pathologists, and other licensed professionals (“  **Licensed Professionals**  ”), and are run in spaces provided by them. These include our  *Early Learning Centres (“*   * **EICs** *   *”)* which are also run in such spaces and focus on early identification and assessment rather than long-duration therapy. The methodology for the identification and onboarding of Licensed Professionals is executed as follows:
a.  Approximately three months prior to the planned inauguration of a new centre, the Company initiates the identification process for suitable Licensed Professionals within the designated city or state. b.  This procedure involves screening, due diligence, and credential verification of potential partners to ensure their alignment with the Company’s quality and compliance standards. c.  Although the Company identifies the cities and states for expansion in advance, the formal engagement of Licensed Professionals is deferred until closer to the rollout timeline. This approach ensures that the most appropriate and currently available professionals are selected for partnership. d.  In support of its expansion strategy, the Company maintains a pipeline of potential Licensed Professionals through ongoing outreach and engagement within the industry. This pipeline allows timely identification and appointment of partners when new centres are being established.
The Licensed Professionals engaged with our Centres, comprising, but not limited to, paediatricians, developmental paediatricians, child psychiatrists, occupational therapists, and speech-language pathologists, serve a dual critical function.
