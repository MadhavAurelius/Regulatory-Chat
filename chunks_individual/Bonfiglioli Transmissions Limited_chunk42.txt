## **Summary of Industry**
The global industrial power transmission market comprises gearboxes and gear motors used across diverse industries for efficient mechanical power transfer. The global industrial gearboxes market is projected to grow from US$ 8.7 billion in Calendar Year 2024 to US$ 11.5 billion by Calendar Year 2030, while the gear motors market is expected to rise from US$ 5.3 billion to US$ 7.2 billion over the same period. The global off-highway mobility solutions (drives) market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.8% between Calendar Year 2024 and Calendar Year 2030, while the global wind turbine drives market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.3% in the same period.
